## Why Aquamarine? The Allure of the Mediterranean's Gem

But why aquamarine, you might wonder? This gemstone is more than just a pretty face in the world of jewels. Renowned for its calming and soothing properties, aquamarine has been cherished throughout history. It’s believed to invoke tranquility and clarity, making it a natural choice for Pomellato's craft.

The deep allure of this blue gem is not just due to its physical beauty but also the stories and legends that accompany it. Sailors of old valued aquamarines as talismans for safe passage and luck across treacherous waters. Today, wearing such a gem is like carrying a piece of history-a link to an age where myths and reality intertwined.

## Pomellato's Milanese Signature: Where Heritage Meets Modernity

As a maison founded in 1967, **Pomellato** has grown to become an emblem of Italian craftsmanship, celebrated for its creativity and innovation. Over the decades, the brand has introduced bold design ideas while remaining true to its heritage, a tradition proudly displayed in each piece of the High Jewelry Collection.

The Aquamarine Dream is a perfect illustration of Pomellato's ability to transcend time, mirroring Milan's eclectic charm and its unwavering dedication to artistry. Each component, from the stones to the intricate settings, is thoughtfully curated to deliver not just a piece of jewelry but a work of art.
