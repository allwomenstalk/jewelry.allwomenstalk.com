The _Art Deco_ movement, known for its **geometric shapes, bold lines**, and luxurious materials, has influenced generations of designers. Originating in the 1920s, Art Deco is synonymous with elegance and sophistication-perfectly embodied by the Harry Winston Emerald Collection.

The eight-sided silhouettes of these watches are more than just aesthetic marvels; they are a nod to an era that celebrated individuality and opulence. The bold symmetry is both visually striking and unmistakably Harry Winston, capturing the spirit of an iconic history in each tick of the clock.

The _black and white colorways_ of the Emerald Collection speak to versatility and timeless appeal. Whether worn to a high-profile gala or a simple brunch with friends, these watches stand out without overpowering.
