# 7 Jewelry Trends to Take Note of ...

Jewelry trends seem to change each season. One day it’s all about sleek metallics and the next we’re all clamouring over neon! Whatever the case, there’s no denying that updating a basic outfit with the latest jewelry trend is an effective way to keep up with the latest fashions. Are you in need of a seasonal update? Then the following are a few fashionable jewelry trends you might want to take note of.

## 1. Oversized Everything

![Oversized Everything](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/jewelry-12_1600x1108.jpg)

Bigger is definitely better, if recent runway collections are any indication of upcoming jewelry trends. From oversized pearl bracelets at Chanel to oversized hoop earrings at Balmain and Diane von Furstenberg, it’s all about the upsize right now. Huge pieces of jewelry can not only spice up your outfit, but they are a big trend right now. When updating your jewelry collection, keep an eye out for those larger than life pieces!

## 2. Choker Necklaces

![Choker Necklaces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/top-jewelry-5_800x1037.jpg)

Necklace lengths have been getting shorter and shorter over the past few seasons. Ditch those collar necklaces and take it one step further with a chic choker. Chokers are a big jewelry trend right now! Keep the look truly contemporary by sticking to thicker, wider styles that really pack a punch. Anything too thin will seem like a 1990s throwback.

## 3. Statement Earrings

![Statement Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/top-jewelry-8_594x800.jpg)

In keeping with current Baroque-inspired trends, pick out a pair of opulent looking earrings. Think plenty of gemstones and heavy chandelier shapes. Chandelier earrings are one of the top trends for jewelry right now. While they might not necessarily be the most comfortable to wear (think of your poor ear lobes), they definitely have that wow factor!

## 4. Tassel Pendants

![Tassel Pendants](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/tl_660x1000.jpg)

Tassel details come and go in many forms when it comes to jewelry trends. A few seasons ago it was all about the tassel earrings and now it’s going to be about the tassel pendant necklace. Take inspiration from the runways of Yves Saint Laurent and choose a tassel pendant that gives off a glamorous 1970s vibe. How cute will that be paired with something a little tough, say a leather moto jacket and a baby doll dress!

## 5. Back in Black

![Back in Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/bl_500x570.jpg)

One of the hottest jewelry trends from recent fashion weeks is jet black jewelry. From black leather cuffs at Celine to the black Madonna-era rosary beads at Jean Paul Gaultier, black is definitely the ‘new black’ when it comes to fashionable trends. Take your black jewelry one step further and look for something with gold or silver accents to really up the glam factor.

## 6. Graphic Cuffs

![Graphic Cuffs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/cuff_554x554.jpg)

It’s still all about the arm party, but instead of stacking on the bangles and bracelets, try a single graphic cuff instead. Designers like Hermès, Fendi, and Missoni were all about the big, graphic cuff when it came to accessorising their recent runway collections. If you want the look a little more polished instead of fun, look for something with a little bit of shine or sparkle for added dimension. Graphic cuffs are a true hit and a big jewelry trend at the moment!

## 7. Animal Instincts

![Animal Instincts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/11/p_800x533.jpg)

Animal motifs are another one of the big trends to take note of right now. Whether it’s in the form of a panther cuff like at Roberto Cavalli, or tiger claw pendants at Kenzo, it’s all about getting in touch with your animal instincts. So don't hesitate to try out a fiercely inspired animal jewelry design! The wilder the jewelry design, the better!

Do you see how bold jewelry trends can be? With a little bit of inspiration from the latest designer runways, these are just a few trends you might want to check out right now. The best part about trend-based jewelry is that it’s an easy way to update an outfit without having to overhaul your entire wardrobe. What jewelry trends are you loving right now?
