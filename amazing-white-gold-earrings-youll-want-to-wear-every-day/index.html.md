# 7 Best White Gold Earrings to Wear Everyday for Any Occasion

**White Gold Earrings** are a chic choice of everyday jewelry, but they're also great for special occasions. If you’re not a fan of traditional yellow gold jewelry, white gold is a stylish alternative. From studs to hooks, there are a range of **white gold earrings** available. While they may look like silver jewelry, white gold jewelry tends to be more expensive. Take a look at some wonderful **white gold earrings** below.

| Earring Style | Best For | Material |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Spike Studs | Adding Edge | 14k White Gold |
| Wishbone | Good Luck Charm | 19k White Gold |
| X-Studs | Modern Minimalism | 18k White Gold |

## 1. Anito Ko Spike White Gold Earrings

![Anito Ko Spike White Gold Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v4/v0/tpbw9ne654c9363d4d999514977322_300x241.jpg)

**Price:** $325.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/g4QQM8)
These stud earrings are great if you want to add some edge to your everyday jewelry. They’re made from 14-karat white gold and feature pyramid stud faces. They also have a slightly distressed finished and a butterfly fastening. Despite being a bit edgier, I think these earrings are still subtle enough to be worn every day. If you like the unconventional vibe of these spikes, you should definitely check out the #7 skull earrings later in this list.

## 2. Jennifer Meyer White Gold Wishbone Earrings

![Jennifer Meyer White Gold Wishbone Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/jg/zc834v9954c9363e0fa46098389374_300x223.jpg)

**Price:** $275.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](https://www.saksfifthavenue.com/)
Add a bit of luck to your jewelry collection with these white gold earrings. They have an offbeat wishbone design and are made from 19-karat white gold. They have a post fastening back with a 9mm drop. They are just as delicate as the #3 studs if you prefer a smaller profile.

## 3. Ileana Makri X-Stud Earrings

**Price:** $1090.00 at [neimanmarcus.com](https://www.neimanmarcus.com/)
X marks the spot with these stunning white gold earrings. These X-shaped 18-karat gold earrings are set with pave diamonds. The design is less traditional than most earring designs, but it’s something that I think works quite well in a modern setting.

## 4. Wouters & Hendrix Diamond Cluster Earrings

![Wouters & Hendrix Diamond Cluster Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/z1/vsrz2o2x54c9363f82995348815876_300x175.jpg)

**Price:** $1431.50 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These diamond cluster earrings can take you from day to night. They have 18-karat white gold hooks with diamond clusters at the drop. They also have a clutch back fastening. However, the price tag on these white gold earrings will definitely be leaving you more than a little bit out of pocket.

## 5. Charles Garnier White Gold Earrings

![Charles Garnier White Gold Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/j2/ylddqqw754c9364077624172500944_272x387.jpg)

**Price:** $545.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These earrings have a simple and chic hoop design. These skinny hoop earrings are made from 18-karat white gold and have a hinge closure. The hoop design is more eye-catching than subtler stud designs. The design is both understated and modern.

## 6. Solange Azagury-Partridge White Gold Diamond Earrings

![Solange Azagury-Partridge White Gold Diamond Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zd/l8/jd3uxfoe54c9364143124300670797_256x294.jpg)

**Price:** $12,200.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/19Jz7z)
Are you sitting down? Because the price of these white gold earrings are sure to knock the wind right out of you. These earrings have a simple hoop design, but are encrusted with diamonds. They’re guaranteed to add some sparkle to an outfit, especially if you are pairing them with a sleek white suit.

## 7. Ileana Makri Skull Earrings

**Price:** $1290.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Xcy8Bn)
Add an unexpected touch to your outfit with these skull earrings. These 18-karat white gold earrings are embellished with 0.05-karat diamonds. They also have designer stamped details at the rear and bell-back fastenings. Work a rock-inspired look and team these earrings with plenty of leather and leopard print.

**Benefits of Choosing White Gold:**

- Highly durable and resistant to scratches compared to silver.

- Provides a neutral, sophisticated backdrop for diamonds.

- Perfect for everyday wear due to its timeless appeal.

As you can see from this list, **white gold earrings** can get a bit pricey. If you like any of these **white gold earrings**, but want something similar on a much lower budget, keep an eye out for silver jewelry in similar designs.
