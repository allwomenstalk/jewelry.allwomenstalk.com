# 7 Affordable Websites for Buying the Best Jewelry around ...

It doesn't matter if you wear the same outfit twice a week if you change it up by pairing it with different jewelry. You can create the illusion of an entirely different outfit and no one will have a clue. That's the beauty of bling! Here are some affordable websites for buying adorable jewelry:

## 1. Shop Miss a

Do you love the dollar store? Well, then you'll love [shopmissa.com](http://www.shopmissa.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com), because everything on the site is only $1. Shipping isn't even expensive (it's $3.95, no matter how much you buy), so you're definitely not going to hurt your wallet. Not only does this site have plenty of necklaces, rings, bracelets, and earrings, but it also has nail-art, cosmetics, and sunglasses. If you don't have much money to spend, but still want to look fabulous, then try ordering from this site.

## 2. Urban Peach Boutique

_Urbanpeachboutique.com_ has jewelry, wallets, bags, and even clothes. There are plenty of statement necklaces and trendy earrings, some of which are under $10. If you're looking for jewelry that can turn your plain outfits into outstanding ones, then you need to check out this website as soon as you can. It'll make you feel like a fashionista.

## 3. Bling Jewelry

The jewelry at [blingjewelry.com](https://www.shareasale.com/notactive.html?389818) is a bit more expensive than the previous sites, but they're still entirely affordable. The site sells toe rings, men's rings, and even engagement rings and wedding bands. They have everything under the sun, so you're bound to find something perfect for you.

## 4. Go Jane

If you only have the time and patience to visit one of these sites, make it this one. [Gojane.com](https://goo.gl/p7VM9o) is perfect for gals who want to stand out. In addition to traditional styles of jewelry, they sell body chains and bracelets that connect to rings. If you're the type to cover yourself in bling, then you'll need to check out this site. You'll find plenty of pretty things to wear around town.

## 5. World Market

Do you hate wearing popular things that every other girl you know owns? Then you should head to [worldmarket.com](https://www.worldmarket.com/category/gifts/clothing-jewelry/jewelry.do), because they have some pretty unique jewelry. The site mostly consists of statement pieces, so if you want to buy something that'll make you stand out, this is the place where you'll find it. Plus, the prices are pretty low, so you don't have to hesitate to fill your cart.

## 6. Baublebar

[Baublebar.com](http://www.Baublebar.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com) allows you to get 15% off of your first purchase. The site has every kind of jewelry that you could ask for, and even allows you to buy personalized items. There are necklaces with names on them, birthstones, and even zodiacs. If you're looking to buy a special gift for a friend, then you should surprise them with something from this site.

## 7. Kay Jewelers (Clearance)

Believe it or not, Kay Jewelers is actually affordable when you check out their clearance section. [Kay.com](https://goo.gl/rx0Cg5) has jewelry with amazing quality for less than $100. It sounds like a lot of money, but when you're buying something that's expertly made, it's worth the money.

The right jewelry can make or break your outfit. That's why you can never have enough rings, bracelets, and necklaces. Jewelry is a girl's best friend, after all. What websites do you usually visit when you want to buy new jewelry?
