# 8 Opulent Drop Earrings ...

Statement earrings are an excellent way of accessorising an outfit. Large drop earrings will add a touch of glamour to any outfit. Keep an eye out for earrings that are encrusted with jewels or feature large gemstones. The look is tastefully extravagant. The following are just a few examples of opulent drop earrings.

## 1. Kenneth Jay Lane 22-Karat Gold-Plated Coin Earrings

![Kenneth Jay Lane 22-Karat Gold-Plated Coin Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sj/ou/wys6so2b54c92b9be4983400988250_267x397.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ctT6Rl)
The hammered finish of these coin-shaped earrings gives these opulent earrings an off-beat vibe. These earrings have a hook fastening and three tiered circular discs at the drop. These earrings could easily be incorporated into both daytime and nighttime outfits. Team these with an ethnic inspired outfit and beaded garments.

## 2. Topshop Stones and Circle Drop Earring

![Topshop Stones and Circle Drop Earring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oo/tc/ob7rs0py54c92b9cd44e5334905611_391x400.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/TVUGNT)
Work a bohemian inspired look with these opulent drop earrings. They consist of several circular discs with turquoise and brown stones. The earrings have a matte gold-look finish. Wear these earrings with a crisp white dress and turquoise accessories for a fresh summer look.

![Asos Vintage Style Style Structured Enamel Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/mw/z2t1dd5z54c92b9dc0e92338525652_344x400.jpg)

Vintage inspired designs are a popular choice. These affordable earrings are a sure fire way to add some vintage glam to your next party outfit. They feature a contrast enamel and gold-tone design and have a bullet back fastening. These earrings would look great with a full-skirted black dress.

## 4. Topshop Metal Facet Drop Earrings

![Topshop Metal Facet Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/o6/bonv4qpk54c92b9edc014603510396_261x399.jpg)

![Dannijo Majorie Silver-Plated Swarovski Crystal Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/b1/aflpkinv54c92b9fa409b997937856_213x400.jpg)

These earrings also have a butterfly fastening at the back. These opulent drop earrings will no doubt add a touch of sparkle to an elegant evening gown.

## 6. Dannijo Jelani Silver-Plated Swarovski Crystal Drop Earrings

## 7. Asos Domed Nugget Drop Earrings

![Asos Domed Nugget Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/hd/eqvb020154c92ba19e5f7043530766_374x398.jpg)
