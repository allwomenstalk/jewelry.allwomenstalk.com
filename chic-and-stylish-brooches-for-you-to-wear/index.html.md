# 9 Chic and Stylish Brooches for You to Wear ...

Stylish brooches are becoming a most fashionable accessory. They’re great for pinning onto blazers and sweaters, and can really add the finishing touches to your outfit. Whether you prefer opulent designs or something more fun and modern, there’s a brooch to suit every taste. Take a look at the following chic and stylish brooches.

## 1. Rhinestone Brooch

![Rhinestone Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/1_rhinestone-brooch_236x528.jpg)

Stylish brooches like these rhinestone ones are a quick way to add some sparkle to your outfit. You can choose between unique antique finds to cheap chain store buys. This set of two rhinestone brooches costs around $14 from ASOS. A cool way to wear these brooches together would be to pin them onto the collar points of a button-up shirt.

## 2. Floral Brooch

![Floral Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/2_floral-brooch_216x320.jpg)

Flower motifs are popular when it comes to brooch designs. They’re beautiful and can add a delicate touch to an outfit. If you’re prepared to fork out the big bucks, designers like Yves Saint Laurent, Sonia Rykiel, and Chanel all make wonderful floral brooches. For those of us on a budget, however, this $7 Forever 21 number is much more appealing!

## 3. Decadent Brooch

![Decadent Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/3_decadent-brooch_393x876.jpg)

Make a statement in an oversized, decadent brooch. This one from ASOS has a very vintage design with a smooth stone centre and embellished chain tassels. With oversized brooches like this one, try wearing it at the centre of a button-up shirt, pinning it close to the top button.

## 4. Color Burst Brooch

![Color Burst Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/4_color-burst-brooch_600x573.jpg)

There are plenty of colorful brooches to fall in love with right now. They’re a great way of working some color into your outfits and can come in a range of different designs. Brooches like this Forever 21 number have a very classic, almost vintage design. If you’re willing to spend a bit more money, Kate Spade and Marni brooches are also worth a look.

## 5. Bug Brooch

![Bug Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/5_bug-brooch_600x631.jpg)

While you might not be a fan of the real deal, in brooch form the bug is quite stylish. This Sonia Rykiel ladybug brooch is a steal at $25 at The Outnet. You can find a range of critter brooches by designers like Oscar de la Renta and Lanvin or at stores like Nordstrom.

## 6. Bow Brooch

![Bow Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/6_bow-brooch_600x869.jpg)

A large fabric bow brooch, like this one from Topshop, is an easy way to add some extra feminine flair to your outfit. You could also easily make a fabric bow brooch of your own. Simply tie a strip of fabric or ribbon into a bow and then attach on with a safety pin.

## 7. Pearl Cluster Brooch

![Pearl Cluster Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/7_pearl-cluster-brooch_424x304.jpg)

The price of a pearl brooch can vary depending on the quality of the pearl and its setting. This chic brooch by Nina features faux pearls, which keeps the cost down. It can be purchased at places like Nordstrom and Zappos, and you’ll be sure to find similar styles of pearl brooches across a range of jewellery stores.

## 8. Profile Brooch

![Profile Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/8_profile-brooch_350x452.jpg)

This pin from Modcloth features the side profile of one very chic lady. It could easily add some quirkiness to a plain cardigan. For something more classic, you could always opt for a vintage-inspired cameo brooch instead.

## 9. Leaf Brooch

![Leaf Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/9_leaf-brooch_355x324.jpg)

Take a cue from nature and add a leafy touch to your outfit. This gold leaf brooch by Brooks Brothers has a classic, almost antique feel to it. It would look great worn with a party dress or pinned onto a chic blouse.

If you want to add a bit of extra spark to your outfit, try a brooch on for size. What are your thoughts on the brooch as an accessory this season? Which of these brooches do you like best?
