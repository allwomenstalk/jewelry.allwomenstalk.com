# 7 Must\-Have Jewelry Pieces for Spring ...

Warmer weather means lower necklines, shorter hemlines and shorter sleeves, so now is the perfect time to start stocking up on jewelry pieces for spring and the upcoming summer season. I love to pile on bracelets when I wear my favorite spring clothes. Necklaces and earrings are fun too! If you’re ready to accessorize for the warmer seasons, you’ve come to the right place. This list of jewelry pieces for spring will get you started, but don’t stop with these great choices. Add to your collection whenever you can and you’ll always have the best look everywhere you go.

## 1. Devon Leigh Red Coral Chandelier Earrings

![Devon Leigh Red Coral Chandelier Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jh/vu/0064z5ix_494x490.jpg)

These earrings are a splurge at $450, but you won’t be sorry to add them to your jewelry pieces for spring. The lovely gold tops with cascading red beads hanging down are sure to become your new favorite. These beautiful earrings work with a variety of colors so you can pair them with most everything in your closet.

## 2. Being Zhenya Necklace

![Being Zhenya Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qj/tf/wx8qicpl_600x423.jpg)

Sure, it’s made with imitation gems, but this vintage inspired necklace will add just what you need for spring this year.The lovely combination of colors makes this the perfect accessory for all your spring outfits.

## 3. Turquoise Teardrop Statement Necklace

![Turquoise Teardrop Statement Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ky/id/ktmye481_500x459.jpg)

If you love a good statement necklace, this is your best bet for spring. It features a lovely cascade of turquoise teardrops that will add that little something extra to your favorite white or yellow sundress. It’s not too big to wear to work, but it also adds a lot to your weekend outfits. You’re going to love this necklace as much as I do – I promise! The really low price tag makes it even better!

## 4. Stella and Dot Florence Stackable Bands

![Stella and Dot Florence Stackable Bands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/ur/bn1gocpy_320x190.jpg)

This set of three rings is the perfect combination of colors for your spring collection. White, green and blue combine to create a stacked but coordinated look you can leave on all spring. The bands are a gold color (but not real gold), and at only $49, you can feel good about this set of rings.

## 5. Palm Springs Vintage Charms Watch

![Palm Springs Vintage Charms Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/cv/wim46nfy_336x381.jpg)

If you’re the kind of girl that never leaves home without a watch, this is sure to become your new favorite. It’s the perfect combination of function and fun. The trendy wrap design combines a soft band with some bling to add jazz to your wrist no matter where you’re going for the day.

## 6. Stella and Dot Georgie Statement Necklace

![Stella and Dot Georgie Statement Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/jl/zfs1ivp1_600x892.jpg)

If you’re looking for something with some sparkle, you should check out this $228 necklace. It combines a great set of sparkling clear stones with gold accents. It looks great with the lower necklines that spring often brings and is sure to bring lots of compliments.

## 7. Maiyet Cuff

![Maiyet Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/xh/hg6w8ku8_600x554.jpg)

If you love the tribal look of spring 2014 and you’re ready for a big time splurge, this cuff will definitely do it for you. At $1,450, it doesn’t come cheap, but it does promise to add so much to your outfit that you’ll wear it every day. Sometimes spending all that dough can be scary, but if you have it and you really want this cuff, why not add it to your spring 2014 collection?

Do you wear expensive jewelry or do you prefer costume pieces? I have kids so I don’t wear my pricey stuff too often, but I do love wearing the good stuff now and then. Otherwise, I stick to great looking, but cheaper stuff for everyday. Do you have a piece for spring that belongs on this list?
