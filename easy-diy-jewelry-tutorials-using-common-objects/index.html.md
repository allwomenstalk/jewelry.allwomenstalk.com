# 7 Easy DIY Jewelry Tutorials Using Common Objects ...

Using common objects to make easy DIY jewelry has begun to catch on. It’s spread from just merely being among the teenage population. The ideas are brilliant and the jewelry can add a little bit more flair to your outfit! You’ll be proud that you created something worth wearing! (And you'll probably get a lot of compliments on it too!) Therefore, I’ve picked seven of my absolute favorite easy DIY jewelry tutorials to share them with you – I hope you like them!

## 1. Soda Can Tab Bracelets

This is an old one, but it’s still one of the most popular easy DIY jewelry tutorials! It's super easy to do! All you need is a colored ribbon and some soda can tabs. No artistic talent needed here! All you need to do is thread the ribbon through the holes in the soda can tab and you're good to go!

## 2. Safety Pin Bracelet

This one is my favorite! I can’t wait to try it out! It looks so chic, especially paired with other bracelets! All you need is some string, colored beads and some safety pins! You can use one color, two colors or many colors to achieve a rainbow look! The decision is yours! Get those creative juices flowing!

## 3. Washer Necklaces

You can use any size washer, especially if you like smaller, less bulky necklaces like me! But if you just follow the directions outlined in the video, you’ll have a really cute pendant necklace that you can wear yourself or give as a gift!

## 4. Zipper Heart Necklace

Who would have thought you could make a heart shaped necklace out of a zipper? You totally can and it looks so cool! This tutorial goes through the process step by step because it isn’t the easiest project to complete but the end result is so worth the extra effort, don’t you think?

## 5. Indian Corn Necklace

I never would have thought about making a necklace out of corn, but it looks similar to beads! How neat is that? All you have to do is thread the corn through a needle and string them all together! It’ll create a bohemian look in no time! Check out the tutorial above for easy to follow instructions!

## 6. Duct Tape Flower Rings

Duct tape is so versatile! It can be used in so many ways and comes in a variety of colors! I had a friend who used to make duct tape wallets, but you can also make duct tape jewelry. This ring is a perfect example. It’s cute and creative and all you need is one material! Would you be willing to try it out?

## 7. Starburst Wrapper Bracelets

These are quite common, so you’ve probably seen them around. You chain link the Starburst wrappers together. I knew a few people who made these in their free time, but I never tried it. They do look super cute though. The colors are so bright and vivid! You can also make these bracelets using other types of candy wrappers!
