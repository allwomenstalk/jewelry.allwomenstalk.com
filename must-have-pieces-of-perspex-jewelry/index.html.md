# 7 Must\-Have Pieces of Perspex Jewelry ...

Perspex jewelry is being touted as a big trend to watch out for this spring. And while there are dozens of must-have pieces of Perspex jewelry on my own little list, I think the best way to go about this trend is big and bold; really make a statement and look for colorful designs and off-beat shapes. Below are a just few of my favorites... here are the [must](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/health-trend-nutrients-in-white-strawberries-you-must-be-getting/)-have pieces of Perspex jewelry on my list!

## 1. Marni Beaded Necklace - Ultra-Chic Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry

![Marni Beaded Necklace - Ultra-Chic Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/ao/bbs833zn54c92ded9a3fc342498616_352x400.jpg)

**Price:** $485 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/GnePPQ)
There is no doubt that Marni works this trend the best. This necklace manages to be elegant and eye catching. The green Perspex offsets the white beads and black ribbon perfectly. The black canvas ribbon means you can easily adjust the length of the necklace, which I think is such a great idea. Wear it with complementary colors and against a relatively blank canvas to really make it stand out.

## 2. Lucy Peacock Perspex Necklace - 1980s-inspired Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry

![Lucy Peacock Perspex Necklace - 1980s-inspired Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/oj/pz6i21qc54c92dee5f63c140939142_325x384.jpg)

**Price:** $237.00 at [farfetch.com](http://www.farfetch.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The geometric design of this necklace is what really stood out for me. I also am quite fond of the color combination of blue, grey and cream. The metal fastenings holding the pieces or Perspex are not only functional, but they look good as well. The twisted rope chain tops the entire design of quite nicely. I’d be glad to have this piece in my wardrobe!

## 5. Sportmax Blue Valdai Perspex Geo Necklace - Girly-girl Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry

![Sportmax Blue Valdai Perspex Geo Necklace - Girly-girl Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nm/bj/jxki3fdf54c92def168ff810163631_259x352.jpg)

**Price:** $156.75 at [my-wardrobe.com](https://goo.gl/X7kQek)
This necklace from Sportmax is dark and edgy. The multiple chains add a heavy edge to the overall design. The flower pendants, however, are nicely juxtaposed against the chain details. Wear it with a floaty dress to make the most of the soft/tough juxtaposition, or alternatively, wear it with more black to really get that edgy look.

## 6. Richard Nicoll Perspex and Metal Necklace - Military-inspired Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry

## 7. Miu Miu Apple-embellished Plexiglass Bangle - Sweet as Candy Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry

![Miu Miu Apple-embellished Plexiglass Bangle - Sweet as Candy Must-Have Piece of Perspex Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ir/tn/cuaom7a854c92defd426a147534911_400x232.jpg)

If necklaces aren’t your thing, you can always try a thick bangle. This Miu Miu Perspex bangle comes in the sweetest green color and features a cute apple-shaped charm. Swarovski crystals are studded around the entire length of the bangle, adding some extra oomph to the design. Style this with anything and everything. It doesn’t matter if it matches or not, it’s bound to make a statement either way! All of these are reasons why this bracelet tops my list of must-have pieces of Perspex jewelry!
