# 8 Cool Marc Jacobs Watches ...

My boyfriend is an avid watch aficionado, always on the lookout for a new one to add to his collection. Naturally, since he loves watches, I’ve started to pay more attention to them too, and I’ve recently discovered the ones that Marc Jacobs offers. They run the gamut from casual to dressy, formal to flirty, and now I want one! Here are 8 cool Marc Jacobs watches…

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Link Bracelet Watch/Stainless Steel

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Link Bracelet Watch/Stainless Steel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e2/wi/kkzjs5p854c8f4fd0d2f1730181584_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This silver and white watch is classic, timeless (no pun intended) with a study link band and a round face. It features an hour, minute, and second hand, and the Marc Jacobs signature on the bezel and on the face. It’s quite understated compared to many o f the other Marc Jacobs watches.

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Ladies Molly Stainless Steel Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Ladies Molly Stainless Steel Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/j2/hrguhzod54c8f4fdd5eb1588191997_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I don’t typically like the look of watches with leather bands, but this one’s gorgeous, and so much fun! It features a lot of shiny brass hardware, with a round face and the Marc Jacobs signature on the face. It’s available with a dark brown leather band, or with a white one, and both are beautiful! The white one would be great for wearing in summer.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Patent Leather Watch/Rosie

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Patent Leather Watch/Rosie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ce/hz/zyzsrbvy54c8f4fed0b74832949326_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Think pink! This bold-looking watch features a round face with the Marc Jacobs signature along the bezel and a magenta patent leather strap. Pair with a grey jumper and patent peep-toes for the office, or with a short sequined skirt and ankle booties for a night out. I love the color!

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Kiss Print Patent Strap Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Kiss Print Patent Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qs/u9/vb7up5iq54c8f4ff9b3de057343780_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $150.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Look at all of those smooches! There must be one for every hour of the day. At first I thought they were a cheetah print… but either way, I love them! The watch is sleek black and white with the Marc by Marc Jacobs signature around the bezel and a patent leather strap.

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs Pelly Silicon Watch/Black & Fuchsia

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Pelly Silicon Watch/Black & Fuchsia](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p7/aj/p3u9zpcw54c8f50078332146127539_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $200.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Any woman who owns anything black knows it’s best to wear it with a pop of bright color, and this watch is an example of that. It has a silicone-coated stainless steel band in matte black, with a black bezel and face and - there! - fuchsia second, minute and hour hands and numbers and dots to mark the time. What fun!

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Icon Watch/Multi-Color

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Icon Watch/Multi-Color](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/nu/v2tffuha54c8f50151927073883117_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This watch is my favorite, with a silver band, and white face, decorates with a different, colorful, iconic Marc Jacobs character, from sassy little Miss Marc to a kitty, bunny, and owl. This would be my everyday watch, one for wearing to work, school… and play! I love the bright colors and of course, the characters!

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Patent Leather Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Blade Patent Leather Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lx/e4/ikc4qm1654c8f5022aea3610685970_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Here’s another watch featuring the mischievous grin of Miss Marc, but where the last one was colorful and bright, this one’s sleek and black and more than a little punk. The band is black patent leather, and the bezel features the Marc by Marc Jacobs signature. The only spot of color is Miss Marc’s smile in bright, eye-catching red.

## 8. Marc by Marc Jacobs Ladies Gold IP Stainless Steel Henry Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Ladies Gold IP Stainless Steel Henry Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/um/h1/gbltqy2q54c8f503394ff014607418_396x528.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Doesn’t this look elegant and refined? It’s inspired by a dressy men’s watch, with a gold ion-plated band and bezel and a date display by the 3-hour mark. It’s so pretty, and I love the somewhat over-sized, chunky styling.

Those are just a few of the watches that Marc Jacobs offers, and now you know why I really, really want one… at least one! Which of these watches do you like best, and why? Please share!
