# 7 Pretty Pieces from the New Elsa Peretti Mesh Collection ...

I’m nearly fanatical about jewelry from Tiffany, so I’m always on the lookout for new lines from their designers. Recently, Tiffany launched a new collection by Elsa Peretti, and I’m in love! It’s so chic and elegant… here are 7 pretty pieces from the new Elsa Peretti Mesh collection…

## 1. Elsa Peretti Mesh Scarf Necklace

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Scarf Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nb/w2/q9lzsbko54c8faead7fd0151599789_475x438.jpg)

**Price:** $1,250.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How incredibly chic! Can you imagine how stunning this would look set against the fabric of your best LBD? It’s made of sterling silver, elegantly draped, and though it looks like a necklace, it’s actually a scarf. The scarf measures 38 inches long, with a tiny sterling silver bed at each end to prevent snags.

## 2. Elsa Peretti Mesh Pendant

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Pendant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/lg/lhnz9xxd54c8faebc182c679824059_475x404.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Perched atop a diamond-shaped mesh of 18-karat yellow gold is a single round-cut diamonds, glittery and perfect. It measures 0.03 carats, and is the perfect partner for the mesh design. The necklace is delicate, but rich, and is 16 inches long.

## 3. Elsa Peretti Mesh Bracelet

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/au/mu2ttula54c8faecbd96f633039510_475x254.jpg)

**Price:** $825.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I adore this bracelet! It’s very fluid, moving with the contours of your delicate wrist. It’s 6¼ in circumference, so it will fit most wrists, and it’s made of sterling silver. It’s very wide, highlighting the tiny, graceful bones of your wrist in a very flattering way!

## 4. Elsa Peretti Mesh Earrings

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cd/pd/brjc7oux54c8faedb0294818229065_475x313.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These earrings make up for their small stature in loads of style! They’re made of sterling silver mesh, topped with a cultured freshwater pearl and French hook (for pierced ears). They’re less than two inches long, and would look wonderful with an updo and delicate pearl choker.

## 5. Elsa Peretti Mesh Necklace

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ak/je/btuxsx9t54c8faee84cd3615631350_475x378.jpg)

**Price:** $5,000.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This necklace is so intricate and delicate… it’s made of brilliant 18-karat yellow gold mesh, formed into a pointed vee for wearing with low-cut dresses and tops. It may look rigid, but it’s actually very soft and malleable, fluid.

## 6. Elsa Peretti Mesh Earrings

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mm/c8/two0njfr54c8faef6ea36750618213_475x383.jpg)

**Price:** $1,575.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These dangle earrings match the first necklace on this list, with a tiny round-cut diamond at each apex for a total carat weight of 0.16. They’re made of 18-karat yellow gold with French hooks for pierced ears. They measure 3¾ inches long… stunning!

## 7. Elsa Peretti Mesh Tassel Necklace

![Elsa Peretti Mesh Tassel Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/ba/n53cw57o54c8faf05c7cb400453135_475x417.jpg)

**Price:** $3,000.00 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Not subtle, but not overstated, this necklace is the flagship of the Elsa Peretti Mesh collection in my view. Each end of this two-strand necklace is capped with a cultured freshwater pearl, giving the effect of layered tassels. It measures 26 inches long, and is made of 18-karat yellow gold.
