# 19 DIY Bracelets to Create ASAP ...

I love a good DIY project, and I love jewelry, so of course I love DIY bracelets! Here are a few of my fave bracelet DIYs, with easy-to-follow instructions that always yield gift-worthy (and keep-for-myself worth) results. Give these ideas a good try - most of them are so gorgeous and are so easy to make!

However, if you do make one and it does not come out the way you hoped, do not stress yourself out. You always have a plan "B" and can order one online, so with this [helpful tip](https://jewelryjealousy.com/baby-bracelets/) in mind, let's check out the amazing DIY options below!
