# 7 Statement Engagement Rings for the Girl Who Loves to Be Different ...

Even though I’m not engaged, I think that there’s something so awesome and different about statement engagement rings. Whenever I come across them on Pinterest, or even in real life, I love how unique they are. Most people go for the same style and shape of engagement ring, and statement rings don’t seem to abide by any rules. If you’re looking to really branch out and be different before you get engaged, or you’re simply like me and think that statement engagement rings are super interesting, you’ll definitely find something you absolutely love on this list!

## 1. A Vintage Ring

![A Vintage Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/no/s6/yn481j8m_600x600.jpg)

There’s something so gorgeous about vintage [engagement rings](https://www.astteria.com/colored-diamond-jewelry/rings). In my opinion, its one of the ultimate statement engagement rings. It’s so much fun to imagine the story that went on before you owned it, and vintage rings are always so beautifully made and intricate. You may even find a one-of-a-kind ring, which is and even if it’s not, the chances of someone you know having the same vintage ring as you is incredibly rare!

## 2. A Roman Numerals Ring

![A Roman Numerals Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fo/ae/neey0tde_570x653.jpg)

I recently saw this on Pinterest and instantly fell in love with the concept. The ring is shaped into your anniversary in Roman numerals. While it may more so be a ring that you use a wedding ring rather than an engagement ring, due to the anniversary, you could always get the day you started dating as the anniversary in the ring rather than your wedding date!

## 3. A Unique Shape

![A Unique Shape](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/yv/zghs4oh4_499x750.jpg)

Everyone has a round diamond, or a princess cut diamond. If you want a diamond ring but don’t want to be like everyone else, a unique shape is perfect for you. There are so many unique shapes you could get, like a hexagonal shape, or an emerald, or something else entirely. You can still be unique while going the diamond route!

## 4. A Unique Stone

![A Unique Stone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i5/de/na1tyy6w_500x750.jpg)

As rustic weddings become more and more mainstream, there are a variety of other stones out there that make great engagement rings. A simple search on Etsy will pull up a ton of different raw stones and rings that will absolutely enchant you!

## 5. Tree Bark Rings

![Tree Bark Rings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vu/ld/tsgy3i2l_600x532.jpg)

Although this may be more of a wedding ring as well, I think that this would make such a great engagement ring as well. Just like with raw stones, they’ve become much more mainstream just like [rustic wedding](https://wedding.allwomenstalk.com/country-rustic-wedding-theme-ideas/) themes have. Tree bark rings are simple rings that have been etched and detailed to look like tree bark. I’ve seen them sold in sets too, which means that you can use them as wedding rings or engagement rings!

## 6. A Raw Diamond

![A Raw Diamond](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nk/f6/hyha8qws_600x600.jpg)

No matter your wedding theme, a raw diamond is the perfect ring to be a little unique without getting too crazy. A raw diamond looks natural and perfect, especially if you love a more natural style. If so, a natural diamond ring is perfect for you!

## 7. Design Your Own

![Design Your Own](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uu/n4/cg6aa31i_500x677.jpg)

It may seem so expensive and out of your price range to design your own ring, but there are actually a lot of more cost-effective choices available. If you can’t find any engagement rings that you absolutely love, a design-your-own option may be just what you’re looking for!

What’s your ideal, unique, statement engagement ring? Are you going to look into any of these? Let me know in the comments!
