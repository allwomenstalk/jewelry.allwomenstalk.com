# 5 Awesome Tips to Buying Jewelry in Wholesale Lots like a Pro ...

If you intend to purchase jewelry to sell, then it is advisable for you to purchase jewelry in bulk. Doing this will help you save a lot of money and it will also help increase your profit margin. If you are looking to purchase jewelry in wholesale lots, you should take a look at the tips compiled below. The following tips will undoubtedly bring success to your venture.

## 1. Tip 1: Licenses and Paperwork

![eyewear, vision care, glasses, health & beauty, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/cj/qlaj7ybx59c02fad76e1a109308973_600x600.jpg)

In some cases, you will first need to obtain a vendor's license before purchasing jewelry in bulk. Of course, you will have to pay a pretty penny to acquire a vendor's license. In addition to that, you will also be required to submit tax forms every now and then. There are many things for you to consider when dealing with foreign sellers. For instance, you will need to know the mode of payment and know if there are extra charges for handling foreign ID accounts. You will also need to know how long it takes for the shipped products to reach you. Therefore, you must first do your homework before getting into the jewelry wholesale business.

## 2. Tip 2: Know Your Jewelry

![jewellery, finger, nail, fashion accessory, hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gq/wh/xp8n9j0k59c02eda1ba24031804526_600x600.jpg)

You would be a fool to enter the jewelry business without proper knowledge of jewelry. It is necessary for you to understand the jewelry making process and be familiar with the terminology used to describe jewelry. You must be able to differentiate between a chemically enhanced gemstone and a simulated gemstone. After all, you wouldn't want to be swindled or fooled into buying fake merchandise now, would you?

## 3. Tip 3: Check for Authenticity

![jewellery, hand, shoulder, fashion accessory, temporary tattoo,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wf/fk/c28u6dxt59c02efb608c0698049565_600x600.jpg)

When purchasing jewelry, it is always necessary to ask to see a certificate or some form of proof of the quality of the jewelry. You need to know that the jewelry you are buying is of good quality and exactly what the seller says it is.

## 4. Tip 4: Where Should You Make Your Purchase from?

_ You should use the outstanding reach of the internet to figure out the best place to purchase the jewelry. While searching online, it is necessary for you to locate similar pieces on different sites and compare them in regards to their prices, return policies, and shipping policies. You should ideally only purchase jewelry from quality dealers that are listed with Better Business Bureau. When dealing with these online stores, you will most probably be asked to provide your vendor's license number or your tax identification number. ## 5. Tip 5: Make Connections with Local Sellers

![blue, jewellery, fashion accessory, hand, shoulder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f0/b8/m30q0og459c02f32af0c0747542851_600x600.jpg)

So, why is it necessary for you to make connections with reputable local sellers? You need to make connections with them because they can turn out to be extremely helpful in cases of cleanings and repairs. You should be well aware that wholesalers don't usually deal with repair services. No matter how high the quality of the jewelry piece, jewelry is always prone to unexpected breaks or bends.
