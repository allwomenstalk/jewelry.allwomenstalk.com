# 7 Watches to Wear Every Day ...

Style-wise, watches seem to go in and out of favour. For me, wearing a watch every day is a habit. When looking at watches to wear on a daily basis, it’s worth spending a little bit extra for a quality item. For the most part, stick to timeless styles in silver, gold, or rose-gold finishes. Choose a watch that goes with your every day pieces of jewellery. Take a look at the following watches to wear every day.

## 1. DKNY Ceramic Watch

![DKNY Ceramic Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/ph/afr7l2yu54c8fedd5685a101217519_314x383.jpg)

**Price:** $242.06 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/ycBDni)
This sleek ceramic watch is a modern interpretation of a classic design. This watch has a ceramic bracelet strap and a silver-tone case with a ceramic bezel. The linked strap and round clock face is a classic watch shape. This watch would look great paired with a minimalist style outfit.

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Rose Gold-Plated Stainless Steel Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Rose Gold-Plated Stainless Steel Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/y6/z8kr9gfs54c8fede2b1a4328312179_267x375.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/04iLNy)
This chunky rose-gold number is one of my top picks for watches to wear every day. It’s also a great way to add a touch of menswear inspired dressing to your outfit. This watch has a bracelet strap, three sub-dials, and date function. The rose-gold finish is a softer take on more classic yellow-gold watches.

## 3. Emporio Armani Stainless Steel Bracelet Watch

**Price:** $357.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/cdsb2d)
This rectangular watch is a great alternative to those watches with round dials. It is made from stainless steel and has a silver-tone finish. It also has a linked bracelet style band with clip fastening. If you wear silver jewellery then this watch could easily be incorporated into your every day style.

## 4. Michael Kors Gold-Plated Wrap Chronograph Watch

![Michael Kors Gold-Plated Wrap Chronograph Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/pl/cza7xjyk54c8fee005456309134244_308x391.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/b9zfqI)
Watches with leather straps are a timeless choice. This one is given a modern twist in the form of a wrap around strap. The tan leather is complemented by the gold-plated stainless steel hardware. This watch would work well with an effortless outfit of cuffed chinos and striped shirt.

## 5. Timex Easy Reader Leather Strap Watch

**Price:** $60.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
When looking for watches to wear ever day, stick to styles that will easily blend into your daily outfits. The combination of rose-gold and white patent leather here is fresh and youthful. It has a round dial with numeric details, has a buckle closure, and is water resistant to 30 metres. The large dial is perfect for those wanting an easy-to-read watch.

## 6. Guess Black Mini Spectrum Watch

![Guess Black Mini Spectrum Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xa/y9/uj4adtag54c8fee1cfefd208790995_314x384.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/HvW9tx)
This all-black watch looks very sporty. It has a plastic link strap, round frame, and multi-function dials. It is a fairly chunky design and very noticeable. That said, the all-black colourway of this watch could easily complement a range of every day outfits.

## 7. Skagen Ladies’ Leather Strap Watch

![Skagen Ladies’ Leather Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ym/ra/qn7rk0oo54c8fee29f291888631923_261x382.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Some people probably prefer petite watches to wear every day. This watch has a slim, minimalist design. It’s also a more up to date take on the classic leather strap watch. It has a brushed chrome dial and Swarovski crystal indexes.

While most of these watches are well over the $200 mark, the cost per wear would be quite reasonable if you wear them every day. Many of these watches also come in a range of finishes, so there’s bound to be something to suit your personal preferences. Which of these are your favourite watches to wear every day?
