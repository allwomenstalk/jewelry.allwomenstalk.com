# 7 Handsome Mens Wedding Bands from Tiffany ...

Your husband’s wedding band is a symbol of your love and devotion, a constant visual reminder of what you mean to him. So it should be something special, something unique, something that’s made to last as long as your love, and if you’re looking for a wedding band that’s all of this and more, your search should start at Tiffany. Here are 7 handsome men’s wedding bands from Tiffany.

## 1. Tiffany 1837 Band Ring

_ **Price:** $1,100.00 at tiffany.com_
For a man who prefers a more narrow ring, this classic Tiffany design measures 3 millimeters wide, one of the smaller rings they offer. It’s simple and stark, in bright platinum, engraved on the outside with the T&Co emblem and 1837, and is available in whole and half sizes from 4 to 13… which means your wedding band could match his, too.

## 2. Tiffany Wedding Band

_ **Price:** $600.00 at tiffany.com_
This modern wedding band by Tiffany is also made of platinum, eternal, rare, and pure, like your love. The design is sleek and contemporary, wider in the middle, then tapering at the top and bottom. It’s also available in whole and half sizes from 4 to 13, ensuring a perfect fit.

## 3. Tiffany Band Ring

![Tiffany Band Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yp/nh/bkn3sxfs54c8f04bde50f049717425_290x69.jpg)

**Price:** $1,525.00 at [tiffany.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tiffany.com/Shopping/Item.aspx?fromGrid=1&sku=GRP01745&mcat=148208&cid=288177&search_params=s+5-p+4-c+288177-r+-x+-n+6-ri+-ni+0-t+-k+)
Another platinum wedding band, this one slightly wider, more substantial, measuring 4 millimeters wide. It features crisp edges, and is wide enough to be engraved with a message, or your initials, inside.

## 4. Tiffany Three-Row Band Ring

_ **Price:** $575.00 at tiffany.com_
This is more of a traditional men’s wedding band, featuring three narrow rows of rich 18-karat yellow gold. This same design is also available in platinum, so there’s sure to be a match for your wedding band.

## 5. Paloma Picasso True Love 24-Hour Ring

_ **Price:** $1,650.00 at tiffany.com_
This platinum wedding band was designed by Paloma Picasso for her own husband, and it’s gorgeous, featuring 24 evenly-spaced pillars, each representing an hour of the day when she loves him. Like most of these wedding bands, it’s available in whole and half sizes from 4 to 13, so your ring could even be a match to his. How romantic!

## 6. Lucida Band Ring

_ **Price:** $1,950.00 at tiffany.com_
This original design features one brilliant half-carat diamond in the center of a 4 millimeter-wide platinum band, perhaps representing the beauty and enchantment of your love for each other. It’s available in an extended range of size, whole and half, from 3 to 13.

## 7. Atlas Ring

_ **Price:** $1,100.00 at tiffany.com_
While this ring is a little too blingy for my taste, it may be just the thing for your husband-to-be. It’s from the wildly popular Atlas collection, featuring a classic Roman numeral motif in 18-karat white gold and brilliant round-cut diamond at the last number, XII.

Those are just a handful of the gorgeous, unique men’s wedding bands at Tiffany, available to buy or browse online or in their shops. I adore the one by Paloma Picasso, but which do you like best, and why?
