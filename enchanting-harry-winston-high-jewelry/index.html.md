## The Magic Behind the Paraiba's Allure

There's something undeniably mesmerizing about a Paraiba tourmaline. The way it captures and reflects light is reminiscent of Caribbean waters glistening under the sun. The heart of this necklace is a vibrant 29.09-carat Paraiba that seems almost alive with color - a rare find that captures the imagination and stirs the heart.

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/5aa0024e-16ad-4c46-a53d-c8b0d8df0aca_1080x1350.jpg)

Photo: @harrywinston

Historically, Paraiba tourmalines were first discovered in the Brazilian state of Paraíba in the late 1980s. They quickly gained a reputation not only for their exceptional color, often described as electric or neon blue, but also for their rarity and unique copper content. Only a handful of these gems are found annually, making the **Harry Winston** piece even more extraordinary and coveted.

## A Symphony of Light: The Yellow Diamonds

Complementing the Paraiba are 17 square emerald-cut yellow diamonds. Their sparkling yellow hue adds a brilliant contrast that enhances the necklace's vibrant blues. Each yellow diamond is carefully selected and cut to perfection, forming a symphony of light and color that dances beautifully around the central stone.

Harry Winston's artisans meticulously choose each diamond to ensure not just uniformity in color and clarity but also to imbue each piece with a sense of harmony and balance - hallmarks of their legendary craftsmanship.

## The Dance of Mixed-Cut Diamonds

Imagine a gallery of 154 mixed-cut diamonds, each adding its own voice to the enchanting story this necklace tells. The variety of cuts used - round, pear, and marquise - contributes to the piece’s shimmering complexity and elegant flow. The careful arrangement reflects a dedication to detail that only a brand like **Harry Winston** can achieve.

This luxurious use of mixed cuts not only enhances the necklace’s overall brilliance but also highlights the unique features of the Paraiba and yellow diamonds, allowing each element to shine individually while contributing to a cohesive and stunning ensemble.

## Crafting a Legacy

Every piece of jewelry by Harry Winston isn’t merely designed; it’s crafted with history woven into its very fabric. Founded in 1932, the brand has consistently pushed the boundaries of creativity and quality. From the famous Hope Diamond to the stylistic elegance of their current collections, Harry Winston continues to hold a majestic presence in the world of high jewelry.

This necklace exemplifies their commitment to excellence. It represents not just a single moment of beauty but a continued legacy of artistry and innovation - a symbol that can be passed down through generations.
