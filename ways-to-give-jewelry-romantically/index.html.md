# 7 Ways to Give Jewelry Romantically ...

When you are gifting jewelry to a woman, especially an [engagement ring](https://www.brilliance.com/engagement-rings), it’s never a bad idea to brainstorm ways to give it romantically! If you’re going to spend the money on a nice chunk of jewels, it’s better to give it to her with a little more ceremony than the box it came in! If you are stumped and in need of a few ways to give jewelry romantically, keep on reading because I’ve got you covered. You’ll find some lovely suggestions below!

## 1. Dinner Time

If you think that I’m about to tell you to hide the ring in her champagne glass, don’t worry! I have other ways to give jewelry romantically that are a bit more original than that! For this tip, you would either need access to a pottery shop, or a little artistic flair all your own. In between courses, have the ring (or other jewelry item) served up on a plate that has been inscribed with the words of your choice. If you don’t have a pottery shop nearby, consider painting the plate yourself.

## 2. Stuffed Animal

This idea may be one of my favorites for giving jewelry romantically. Buy a stuffed animal, particularly one that you know your special someone will adore. The jewelry can be placed on corresponding parts of the stuffed animal’s body, or you can arrange the bear to look as if he is holding the box the jewelry comes in. Either way, this is sure to be a fantastic hit with the ladies!

## 3. Glass Ornament

Are you giving jewelry as a gift at Christmas time? Why not hide your gift in plain sight and place it in a clear glass ornament bulb! You can hang it on the tree if you like. The lights will catch, and cause the shiny bauble inside to glimmer and gleam so beautifully. This is one Christmas she is sure to remember!

## 4. Everyday Items

Here’s a tip that you can put to good use if you’re a woman looking to give jewelry romantically to a man. Try hiding it in something he uses every day. If he drinks coffee, put it in his coffee cup. If he reads, place it in his book. If he likes to hunt or fish, go nestle the little gift into his tackle or ammunition box. Same thing goes for men using this tip. Choose something she uses on a daily basis and hide your gift where she is sure to find it!

## 5. Work Day

This trick may be an old one, but it will still get you some points! Have your gift delivered while the recipient is at work, along with flowers or chocolates. Everyone likes being spoiled and doted on, and when you make sure others know how special your significant other is to you, you’re sure to score a winning hit!

## 6. Jewelry Box

What better way to gift jewelry than in a jewelry box? I love this cute little idea! Choose a jewelry box according to your lover's taste. You can have a jewelry box custom-made, engraved or refinished, or you could pick up a vintage box from an antique store nearby. You can also choose from scores of pre-made boxes in the store, from dainty and delicate, to sweet and cute, or plain and simple. Go with something that you know will be a hit!

## 7. Sweet and Simple

Who says you need bells and whistles to pull off giving jewelry in a romantic way? If it’s more your style to be plain and simple, then go with plain and simple. Pick up a card, or write one yourself expressing your feelings. Choose an atmosphere that is right, and give your gift with all the love you have!

As you can see, many of these suggestions can work whether you are a man, gifting jewelry to a woman, or a woman, gifting jewelry to a man. Some of these ideas are even cute for friends to use, if you have bought a piece of nice jewelry for a good friend or sibling! What are some of your favorite ways to gift jewelry romantically? Please, share your stories below!
