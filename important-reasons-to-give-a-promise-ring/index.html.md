# 5 Important Reasons to Give a Promise Ring ...

If you are of a certain age, you might remember the media frenzy that once surrounded the Jonas Brothers, when in the height of their boy band fame, they all proudly sported promise rings. In their particular case, it sounds like the promise rings had something to do with keeping their love lives at a PG-13 level, but that isn’t always what a promise ring is used for. In terms of different meanings, a promise ring can pretty much come to symbolise anything you want it to. Here are five different **reasons to give a promise ring**.

## 1. Token of Love

Similar to the old fashioned ‘going steady’ rings, a promise ring can be something that you wear to both show off and display that you are in a committed relationship. Rings are a nice piece of jewellery to receive at any time, so one from a new boo who wants to celebrate the fact that you are an item is never a bad thing, especially if you love your accessories!

## 2. Pre-Engagement

For some couples, it takes long time a lot of reflection and preparation to finally pop the question. However, if you don’t feel quite ready or in the right financial space to really crack out a great engagement ring, you can always use a promise ring in the meantime as a kind of precursor for the real thing. It shows your commitment to one another, but it doesn’t necessarily mean that you are setting a date for the big day just yet.

## 3. Don’t Want Marriage

In an unconventional twist, some people actually wear promise rings as a substitute for anything more official, because for their own reasons, they don’t want to enter into traditional marriage. You might think it’s outdated, or patriarchal, or simply just not for you, but a nice simple promise ring can be a good compromise to display commitment without all of the ceremony and paperwork.

## 4. Faith

Wearing a promise ring can be a way for you to show faith in your partner, faith that you he is the one for you no matter what, and faith that you will be together for a long time yet, hopefully forever. One day, you hope to replace it with a wedding ring, but for now, you are happy to show your love for him in the form of a promise ring instead.

## 5. Promise!

As the name suggests, you can exchange promise rings in order to do just that, keep a promise! For example, promising yourself to a particular person, promising that you will wait for someone to come home, promising anything really!
