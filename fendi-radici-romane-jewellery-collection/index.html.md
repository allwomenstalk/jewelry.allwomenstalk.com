# How Fendi Transforms Roman History into Stunning High Jewellery

Imagine strolling through the eternal beauty of Rome, where every corner tells a story, and the air feels thick with history. This dreamy landscape serves as the perfect backdrop for Fendi's newest masterpiece, the Radici Romane High Jewellery Collection. Created by Delfina Delettrez Fendi, this collection whispers tales of grandeur, a testament to the brand’s legacy and its unwavering creativity. But why does this collection resonate so much with anyone who cherishes both beauty and history?

Delfina, a guardian of her family’s storied past, infuses each piece with memories and timeless allure. And on its 100th anniversary, [Fendi](https://www.instagram.com/fendi/) takes us on a remarkable adventure, weaving through inspirations from Karl Lagerfeld to Roman sunsets. Through this article, we’ll explore not just jewelry, but narratives crafted in gems and metals. Let’s dive into the mesmerizing world of Fendi, where each jewel is more than an accessory-it's an artifact of storytelling, history, and art.

The enchantment starts now. Each piece sparkles with a story begging to be told. Are you ready to discover the wonders of Fendi’s creation? Let’s embark on this dazzling journey together!

## Les Cinq Doigts d’une Main: A Sketch Comes to Life

The journey begins with the **Les Cinq Doigts d’une Main** set. Inspired by a sketch from the iconic Karl Lagerfeld in 1974, this set is a tribute to the five original Fendi sisters. The collection captures their _bold femininity_ and _unwavering creativity_ through five intricately designed rings. Bold and powerful, each ring exhibits a unique gemstone, reflecting the vibrant personalities of the sisters. Priced between $15,000 and $20,000, these rings are a mirror to the past with a polished gleam of the present.

Imagine wearing a piece of history that doesn't just adorn you but also tells a story-these rings do just that. Their varying cuts and colors make them versatile, suitable for both a glamorous night out or a reflective afternoon high tea.

### The Allure of the Roseto Parure

Now, let’s wander through the effervescent charm of the **Roseto parure**. A fusion of elegance and emotion, this set brings together the allure of roses-Anna Fendi’s cherished bloom-with oval-cut topazes reminiscent of Roman sunsets. The hues radiate warmth and softness, like the final rays of a beautiful day. Costing approximately $25,000, this parure feels luxurious yet connected to nature.

**Delicate yet striking,** the Roseto parure has the power to transform any attire into a poetic expression. Whether you're attending a wedding or an art gallery opening, this set becomes an extension of your identity, a whisper of your sophistication.

## Nature’s Symphony: The Nemus Parure

Next, we find ourselves enveloped in the lush splendor of the **Nemus parure**. Composed of 17 perfectly matched oval-cut emeralds, held together by a dense foliage of diamonds, this offers an ode to Roman nature. Personal yet majestic, this set captures the essence of unspoiled natural beauty. Valued around $30,000, it's not just a purchase-it's an investment in art.

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/7bbddd8a-8dd0-472b-b27b-990fda47b7f3_1440x1800.jpg)

Photo: @fendi

Emeralds, known as symbols of rebirth and love, nestle gracefully against the diamonds, intertwining to create something that feels alive. Picture wearing this piece to a grand evening event or a serene dinner-its presence commands quiet admiration and respect.

### Architectural Elegance: The Purpura Set

Finally, our journey through Fendi's Radici Romane High Jewellery Collection brings us to the **Purpura set**. Inspired by Roman architecture, this set entices with a bichromatic play of pavé, showcasing vibrant purple and blue tanzanites. Each piece is an architectural marvel, coveted for its ability to whisper tales of forgotten eras.

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/2e2fd8bf-8948-4c99-b94e-8a6588c1df2a_1440x1800.jpg)

Entering the price range of approximately $40,000, the Purpura set is designed for an individual who cherishes vibrant elegance combined with history. Wearing this collection feels akin to adorning oneself with the heartbeat of Rome itself, where art and life seamlessly intertwine.

It’s evident that Fendi isn’t merely creating jewelry; it’s crafting legacies in wearable forms. The entire collection is as much about heritage as it is about the future. Fendi knows that jewelry isn’t just something you wear-it’s something you embody.
