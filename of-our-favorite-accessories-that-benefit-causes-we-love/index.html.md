# 7 of Our Favorite Accessories That Benefit Causes We Love ...

If you’re looking for some new accessories for fall, why not pick up some accessories that benefit good causes? There are tons of stylish, chic and fun accessories that were made to benefit some awesome charitable organizations so it’s a win-win situation! Whether you buy it for yourself or as a gift for someone else, these accessories that benefit good causes give you a great opportunity to give back.

## 1. Raven + Lily Alem: Curry + Silver Triangle Tassel Necklace

![Raven + Lily Alem: Curry + Silver Triangle Tassel Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23010909_5975_499x652.jpg)

Let’s start off this list of accessories that benefit good causes with a lovely necklace from Raven + Lily. This necklace is super chic and looks fab layered with other necklaces. It’s handmade and made from recycled metals and colored curry thread. This accessory benefits women in Ethiopia living with HIV and helps provide training and jobs.
**Price:** $14 at [ravenandlily.com](http://www.ravenandlily.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Beats by Dre Solo HD Headphones

_ Everyone can use a good pair of headphones and if you’ve got the extra cash, why not spring for a pair that benefits The Global Fund? Part of every purchase benefits The Global Fund to help fight AIDS, malaria and tuberculosis and you get a rad pair of headphones that sound great and are said to be almost impossible to break.
**Price:** $199.95 at beatsbydre.com_

## 3. Greater Good Network Pink Ribbon Touch Screen Gloves

![Greater Good Network Pink Ribbon Touch Screen Gloves](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23010917_9078_599x529.jpg)

Now that fall is in full swing, it’s the perfect time to get some cute gloves to keep your hands warm and shield them from the wind. This charitable accessory that benefits breast cancer awareness not only keeps your fingers toasty, but they still allow you to use touchscreens on your tech accessories, at gas pumps and ATMs so you stay that way!
**Price:** $15.95 at [thebreastcancersite.greatergood.com](http://redirect.viglink.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 4. Angela Moore Autism Society of America Bracelet

![Angela Moore Autism Society of America Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23010908_3890_219x215.jpg)

Angela Moore partnered with various charitable organizations to create some special collections of accessories to benefit their cause. This colorful bracelet was designed to spread awareness about autism. 15% of the proceeds from this charitable accessory goes towards the Autism Society of America. But it doesn’t stop there, you can also get a hair tie, neck tie, necklace, earrings or charm to show your support.
**Price:** $50 at [angelamoore.com](http://angelamoore.com/)

## 5. TOMS Gigi Navy Polka Dot Sunglasses

![TOMS Gigi Navy Polka Dot Sunglasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23010909_2268_499x176.jpg)

I think most of us are familiar with TOMS shoes and that whenever you buy a pair of their shoes, they also donate a pair of shoes to a child in need. Well, they also have cute sunglasses and when you purchase a pair, they also donate a pair of prescription glasses, medical treatment or surgery to help save someone’s sight.
**Price:** $98 at [toms.com](http://www.toms.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 6. Soulscarf Cranberry Infinity Scarf

_ Soulscarf creates casual-chic scarves in a variety of colors and materials that are perfect to add some interest to your outfit or to keep you warm in the chilly months. The great thing about Soulscarf is that you get to pick the charity you want to help! 20% of the cost of the scarf will be donated to either the ASPCA, Feed the Children, Project H Design or The Breast Cancer Society.
**Price:** $44 at soulscarf.com_

## 7. The Animal Rescue Site Key to My Heart Paw Keychain

![The Animal Rescue Site Key to My Heart Paw Keychain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23010910_7984_599x479.jpg)

If you’re passionate about animal welfare, proudly show your support with this charming keychain. Purchase of this keychain provides 14 bowls of food to animals in shelters and sanctuaries. Proceeds are divided between the Petfinder Foundation, Fund for Animals, and International Fund for Animal Welfare (IFAW).

**Price:** $9.95 at [theanimalrescuesite.greatergood.com](https://theanimalrescuesite.greatergood.com/clicktogive/ars/home)

These are just a few of the many fabulous accessories that benefit good causes. Keep an eye out for some great designer-charity collaborations. Most of these collabs are only for a limited time, but you can score great pieces from some hot designers and stores that benefit incredible charitable organizations.
