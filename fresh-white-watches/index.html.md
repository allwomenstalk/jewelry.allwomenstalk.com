# 7 Fresh White Watches ...

All-white watches are a funky way to update your outfit. The look is sporty and youthful. As white is a neutral colour, these watches can be worn with a variety of outfits. They’re a fairly casual choice of accessory. Add a sporty twist to timeless outfits and add one of these fresh white watches to your collection.

## 1. River Island Plastic Watch

![River Island Plastic Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/qe/ij1wl0gg54c92782b8b1a036441004_392x479.jpg)

**Price:** $46.53 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/U0S42p)
This unisex plastic watch has a timeless design. It has a bracelet style strap, a round dial, and silver tone accents to the face. The bracelet strap also has a fold-over clasp fastening. Wear this watch with a navy striped top and white tailored trousers for a nautical-inspired look.

## 2. Michael Kors MK5387 Watch

![Michael Kors MK5387 Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/xg/ubzxqqr654c92783be9c9446171443_392x478.jpg)

**Price:** $825.75 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Y7778s)
This ceramic bracelet features a unique-looking link strap. Fresh white watches can all start looking very similar, so keep an eye out for those with interesting details and features. This watch has a round dial, contrasting indexes, and multi-function dials. It also has a jewellery clasp closure. This watch is quite chunky so keep the rest of your jewellery on that hand fairly minimal.

## 3. Superdry Sport All White Strap Watch

![Superdry Sport All White Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ah/le/kk0fsosw54c92784b3707407552875_392x472.jpg)

**Price:** $68.96 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/Hjh6mD)
This all-white watch has a very sporty appearance. It has a resin case and strap, which features the Superdry logo. This watch also has a round analogue face and adjustable buckle fastening. It is water resistant to 30 metres and will withstand rain or splashes of water.

![Movado Small Bold Crystal Marker Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/lr/veavx1hp54c92785a9b6e838611294_326x438.jpg)

It is also water resistant to 30 metres. Work a monochrome outfit and wear this watch with a black shirt dress and patent white shoes.

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs White Bracelet Watch

![Marc by Marc Jacobs White Bracelet Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/by/pc/qqkrubff54c92786a5489635842533_392x479.jpg)

**Price:** $215.49 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/hiN8oe)
Fresh white watches are quite youthful in appearance. This watch features colourful brand lettering around the face. Each letter corresponds with a different hour. It has a rubber bracelet, round dial, and classic metal pin buckle closure. Wear this watch with a cute denim dress and canvas shoes.

## 6. Nixon the Player Stainless Steel Watch

![Nixon the Player Stainless Steel Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fo/hy/jgdgpo3n54c92787a26de807496494_333x453.jpg)

**Price:** $225.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/o3tqEV)
This white watch has a square analogue dial. It is made from white stainless steel and has gold-tone trims. The watch face has scratch-resistant crystal glass protection. The square dial has a certain retro feel to it. This would make a carefree and casual addition to an outfit.

## 7. Skagen Small Ceramic Watch

![Skagen Small Ceramic Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p6/zo/iu3zg56b54c927887ea22286058353_326x475.jpg)

**Price:** $195.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
What I like most about fresh white watches is their minimalist appearance. This petite ceramic watch features crystallised markers on the dial. It has a bracelet strap with a push-button closure. Wear this watch with a full-skirted pastel dress for a feminine look.

Fresh white watches have a minimalist appeal. They’re a great alternative to gold and silver watches, and are not as heavy as all-black watches. Which of these white watches do you prefer?
