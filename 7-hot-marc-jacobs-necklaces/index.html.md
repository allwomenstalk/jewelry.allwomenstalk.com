# 7 Hot Marc Jacobs Necklaces ...

I love everything Marc Jacobs designs, from tees to snowboards, shoes to jewelry. I especially adore his necklaces - they’re so fun and casual and unique! And they also make great gifts. Here are a few I think you’ll love, too… here are 7 hot Marc Jacobs necklaces…

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Necklace

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/wt/rsq1c0a754c8f4f4c7ecd419972264_396x442.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
One of my favorite MJ characters is little Miss Marc, sassy and adorable and sweet, just like a certain 7-year-old I know. This necklace features a smiling Miss Marc wearing super-cute heart-shaped red sunglasses on a 17-inch silver-tone chain. I’d layer this with other silver necklaces, and wait for the compliments to roll in.

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Enamel Disc Pendant Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/ju/doaxyt1w54c8f4f5a70a1447129007_396x441.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Available in black with silver, or cream with gold, this pendant is simple, elegant, ideal for layering or wearing alone with a casual LBD. It hangs from a 16-inch chain, and is exactly the right length to wear with v-neck tops and dresses. The cream is nice, but I really like the black!

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Nancy Necklace

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Nancy Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/iu/brlzlcqb54c8f4f6bf659192447907_396x463.jpg)

**Price:** $298.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Who knew Marc Jacobs designed statement necklaces? There’s no other way to describe this one, with the darling pink and gold hummingbird hovering between a garden-full of delicate pink resin flowers, all suspended from a gold-tone chain with ribbon woven through. What a pretty way to celebrate spring!

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Grosbeak Fauna Pendant Necklace

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Grosbeak Fauna Pendant Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/ty/caszxwmn54c8f4f791ac9115046680_396x445.jpg)

**Price:** $58.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
As big, bold, and beautiful as that last necklace was, this one is sweet, charming and bright, with a dainty canary on a delicate chain, with a tiny Marc Jacobs charm, too. The overall length is about 18 inches, perfect for wearing with a floral-print dress or just a cream tunic and leggings. I love the yellow!

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Hare Pendant Long Necklace

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Hare Pendant Long Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n8/dy/yfhea29q54c8f4f9319a1749794354_396x459.jpg)
