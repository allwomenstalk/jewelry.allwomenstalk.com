# 10 Statement Necklaces on a Budget ...

Statement Necklaces are just one way to update your new season look. While designer options are out of reach and unrealistic choices for many of us, there are a range of equally stunning yet affordable options on the market. Simply look in your favourite high street or chain store to get your fix of statement necklaces this season. Take a look at a few such statement necklaces below.

## 1. Topshop Sequin Peter Pan Collar

_ **Price:** $28.00 at us.topshop.com_
Collar style necklaces are proving to be popular this season. This necklace has a vintage inspired Peter Pan design and features bronze sequin detailing. Wear this necklace over a black dress with a high neckline for a fun party look.

## 2. ASOS Collar Necklace

_ **Price:** $28.75 at asos.com_
This necklace has a daring design. It features five large enamel tooth shaped pendants in a tiered chain design. It has a bead chain to the reverse with a lobster clasp fastening. Wear this necklace over a simple grey sweater and with blue jeans for a chic everyday look.

## 3. ASOS Pleated Leather Collar Necklace

_ **Price:** $28.75 at asos.com_
Leather necklaces are a great alternative to more common metal statement necklaces. This collar style necklace has wide pleated leather body with a tie closure at the rear. It would look simply chic with an apricot coloured top and tan suede mini skirt.

## 4. ASOS Necklace with Rope Tube Tassle

_ **Price:** $26.96 at asos.com_
This rope necklace would make a crafty addition to any outfit. It has a tiered gold-tone tube pendant with a large tassel detail at the drop. It has an oversized design and would look great teamed with a tribal inspired patterned top.

## 5. Topshop Triangle Cut out Collar

_ **Price:** $35.00 at us.topshop.com_
If you like your statement necklaces bold and daring, you can’t go past this triangle cut out collar. It has a burnished gold finish with triangle cut out designs. The triangle cut outs range from large pendants at the front to smaller pendants at the back. This would look great set against a neutral silk blouse.

## 6. Pieces Pernille Necklace

_ **Price:** $28.75 at asos.com_
This stylish bib necklace has a distinctive hammertone finish. The wide flat snake chain is adjustable and has a lobster clasp fastening. Plain metallic statement necklaces, such as this silver-tone one, are ideal for wearing during both the day and night.

## 7. Topshop Mesh Loop Tassel Necklace

_ **Price:** $25.00 at us.topshop.com_
Add a dazzling finish to your evening wear with this long mesh necklace. It features multiple chains, two hooped pendants, and a tassel detail at the drop. Wear this with a maxi dress with a V-neckline.

## 8. Forever21 Sparkling Collar Necklace

_ **Price:** $12.80 at forever21.com_
Add a sense of sparkling sophistication to your outfit with statement necklaces that feature jewel-like pendants. This necklace features ring and faceted beads, and is accented with an oversized drop pendant. Wear it with your favourite LBD for a statement-making look.

## 9. Forever21 Fringe Necklace

_ **Price:** $6.80 at forever21.com_
Fringed necklaces are great for making a statement. This affordable number features a combination of rope and chain fringing. It is lightweight and would look worn with patterned pieces for an eclectic look.

## 10. Modcloth Chrysanthemum Necklace

_ **Price:** $19.99 at modcloth.com_
Statement necklaces with intricate detailing make for beautiful pieces. This one has a bib design and features an ornate floral pattern. The adjustable chain is perfect for achieving the right length, for a range of necklines. Wear this necklace with all your favourite frocks!

Statements necklaces come in all shapes and sizes. No matter what look you are trying to achieve, there’s bound to be a necklace to suit. This season is all about statement necklaces, so will you be wearing them this season?
