# 7 Tiffany Bracelets I Covet ...

I have long acknowledged my weakness for anything designed by Tiffany, and that includes nearly their entire line, especially their bracelets! Bangles, cuffs, and links, I love them all! Here’s a sampling to show you just why. Here are 7 Tiffany bracelets I covet.

## 1. Tiffany Metro Bangle

_ **Price:** $5,900.00 at tiffany.com_
At first glance, this diamond-studded bracelet looks like a traditional tennis bracelet, but it’s not - it’s a bangle! The band is made of rich 18 karat rose gold, and is made to fit most medium-sized wrists. The round-cut diamonds are brilliant, and measure a total carat weight of 1.73.

## 2. Tiffany Garden Flower Charm Bracelet

_ **Price:** $4,800.00 at tiffany.com_
What a marvelous way to celebrate spring, any time of year! This is the Tiffany bracelet I covet most. The wide-link charm bracelet is made of 18 karat rose gold with little flower-shaped charms with petals made of orange and rose opal, mother-of-pearl, aventurine and blue chalcedony. The bracelet is a generous 8 inches long, so it will fit most wrists

## 3. Tiffany Yours “Love Makes the World Go 'round” Bangle

_ **Price:** $375.00 at tiffany.com_
If you agree with this age-old sentiment, then this romantic little Tiffany bracelet may have been made for you to covet! It’s made of thin sterling silver, finished with delicate milgrain around the edges and stamped with a serif font statement. It’s quite small, designed to comfortably fit wrists less than 6 inches in diameter. Wouldn’t this make a sweet anniversary gift?

## 4. Tiffany Twist Link Bracelet

_ **Price:** $350.00 at tiffany.com_
What a beautiful every day bracelet! It’s made of wide elliptical links, each with a unique twist pattern. The links and over-sized lobster clasp are made of bright sterling silver… you might even use this as a charm bracelet, or just wear it alone… it comes in two lengths (8 or 8¾ inches), so you’re sure to get a perfect fit!

## 5. Tiffany Atlas Bangle

_ **Price:** $400.00 at tiffany.com_
I adore the entire Atlas collection, for its understated elegance and sophistication, but I’m really coveting this bracelet in particular. I love the Roman numerals all the way around, and the silver makes it casual enough to wear every day.

## 6. Tiffany Petals Cuff

_ **Price:** $3,900.00 at tiffany.com_
This cuff bracelet is made of lush 18 karat rose-colored gold, with a simple but elegant open-work flower pattern. At the center of each flower is a sparkling round-cut diamond, adding a little extra shine. And who doesn’t want a little extra shine? It’s also in sterling silver.

## 7. Tiffany Elsa Peretti Wave Three-Row Bracelet

_ **Price:** $475.00 at tiffany.com_
This is one of my favorite of Elsa Peretti’s designs, and simple three-row wave done in sterling silver. The lines are thin and graceful, mimicking your wrist and adding a subtle shine to an LBD or a simple floral-print summer dress.

Isn’t that flower bracelet gorgeous? And this list represents just a handful (or armful) of the bracelets from Tiffany I covet! There are so many more… which of these bracelets do you like best, and how would you wear it? Or is there another Tiffany bracelet you’re currently coveting?
