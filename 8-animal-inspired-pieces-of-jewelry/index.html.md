# 8 Animal Inspired Pieces of Jewelry ...

If you want to add a bit of punch to your overall outfit, try getting your hands on some animal inspired jewelry. This trend has been popular for the past couple of seasons and is still going strong. You can get your fix from both designer and chain-store brands. Just take a look at the following animal inspired pieces of jewelry

This bear ring is an elegant take on the animal jewelry trend. This ring features a ‘kissing bear’ design and has the designer detail stamped on the inside. This ring will definitely add interest to your outfit. It’s also delicate enough to be worn on a daily basis.

## 2. Forever21 Natural Beauty Feather Earrings

![Forever21 Natural Beauty Feather Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s2/d5/x0rqu0bb54c8f582496d3588659826_328x383.jpg)

## 3. Topshop Open Wings Ring

![Topshop Open Wings Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ps/es/ihm0ov4w54c8f58319787481050320_314x389.jpg)

## 4. House of Harlow 1960 Gold Plated Peacock Necklace

![House of Harlow 1960 Gold Plated Peacock Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/db/fv/v43fc7es54c8f583ddfbf213314960_377x400.jpg)

**Price:** $173.92 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/q51Fyo)
This gold-plated pendant is sure to add a bit of sparkle to your outfit. This necklace, inspired by the 60s and 70s, features an oversized peacock pendant with adjustable chain and lobster claw clasp. I’d wear this with complementing jewel toned colours - think deep greens and purples. Play up the 60s/70s look with a floor skimming outfit.

## 5. Alexander McQueen Bird Skull Ring

![Alexander McQueen Bird Skull Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r8/nj/w98pzy6z54c8f584aec3b553059644_336x383.jpg)

**Price:** $295.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/G3D2ym)
This gothic inspired ring is a guaranteed statement maker. This ring is made from silver-toned brass and features a bird skull design. It’s quite a long ring, and covers a great deal of your finger. The overall design is macabre and dramatic, which is typical of the Alexander McQueen aesthetic.

## 6. Aurelie Bidermann Mamba 18-karat Gold-plated Snake Ring

![Aurelie Bidermann Mamba 18-karat Gold-plated Snake Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/h8/azlgkil054c8f5859c869787616808_316x384.jpg)

**Price:** $190.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/UIfvDD)
I love the double headed snake design of this ring. It features a wrap-around style design and snake-effect embossed detailing. This ring is all about casual luxe. Keep the rest of your outfit neutral to really let this ring pop.

## 7. Topshop Cut out Fish Necklace

![Topshop Cut out Fish Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/nr/ssgmo01854c8f586944c8056279836_279x400.jpg)

## 8. Asos Two Tone Metal Scarab Beetle Bracelet

![Asos Two Tone Metal Scarab Beetle Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vs/g9/maojfdfh54c8f5874b774242626304_400x149.jpg)
