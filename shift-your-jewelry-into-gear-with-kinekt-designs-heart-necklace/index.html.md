# Shift Your Jewelry into Gear with Kinekt Designs Heart Necklace ...

You may be familiar with Kinekt Design's famous Gear Ring but now, the company has shifted things into second gear with the release of their latest creation, the Gear Necklace!

The necklace was designed to complement the company's first product and, just like the ring, the necklace has its own unique and interactive movement. It's made from high quality brushed stainless steel and features a ball chain that is threaded through three moveable gears, set within a heart-shaped pendant. The gears are set in motion just by sliding the pendant along the chain or gently pulling on either side of the heart's strings.
