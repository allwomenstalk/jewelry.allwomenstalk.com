# 12 Birthday Gemstones and Their Meanings ...

If you are interested in learning about birthday gemstones and their meanings, there is a wealth of information. Birthstones have been around since ancient times; however, the modern birthstone list was put together in 1912. The American National Association of Jewelers standardized birthstones in 1912, and updated the list in 2002 by adding Tanzanite as a December birthstone. Traditionally, people wore their birthstones to bring good luck, but there is much more to know about birthday gemstones and their meanings.

## 1. Garnet

![Garnet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200437_9120_600x529.jpg)

I must admit to being partial to garnets, since they are my birthstone. Garnets are January’s gemstone, and they are said to be a talisman of protection, ensure success, and possess curative powers. Garnets are typically a deep red or rich purple color, but they come in a variety of colors. The qualities associated with garnets are purity, truth, faithfulness, and friendship. They are also supposed to be healing for problems with the lungs and heart. While I am not sure if garnets possess all these powers, learning about birthday gemstones and their meanings is fascinating.

## 2. Amethyst

![Amethyst](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200522_1927_300x300.jpg)

February’s gemstone is the beautiful amethyst. Amethysts range from light purple to dark purple in color, which is the color of royalty. They are associated with stability, peace, balance, courage, inner strength, sincerity, and a calm disposition. If you are wondering about their healing properties, amethysts are help with headaches, insomnia, arthritis, and pain. Interestingly, they are also considered to be a wonderful gemstone for healing addictions.

## 3. Aquamarine

![Aquamarine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200548_2434_600x387.jpg)

I have always loved the calming sea blue color of March’s birthstone, which is biblically symbolic for heavenly grace. The aquamarine is associated with faithfulness, courage, and friendship. Traditionally, it is also said to enhance awareness and communication. Like all birthstones, the aquamarine has a set of healing powers. These include, healing throat problems and stress related illnesses.

## 4. Diamond

![Diamond](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200614_3911_599x459.jpg)

Diamonds may be a girl’s best friend, but they are April’s birthstone. Not surprisingly, diamonds are supposed to enhance relationships. They also increase inner strength, balance, clarity, and abundance. Diamonds are healing for brain diseases and problems with the pituitary glands. Although they are generally colorless, you can also find colored diamonds. Personally, I love the sparkle of diamonds and treasure my diamond studs that my grandmother gave to me.

## 5. Emerald

![Emerald](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200639_0810_599x459.jpg)

My first introduction to emeralds was through the Wizard of Oz and the Emerald City. I have since learned that emeralds are May’s birthstone, and by weight they are the most valuable gemstone in the world. Emeralds are associated with memory, clairvoyance, and faith. Their beautiful green color is symbolic for renewal of life and nature, and they are healing for the eyes, spine, and fertility.

## 6. Pearl

![Pearl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200726_3594_599x537.jpg)

Traditionally, June’s birthstone represented modesty, chastity, and purity, which is probably explained by its popularity in the Victorian Era. The pearl is also supposed to enhance personal integrity. Pearls take 5 to 8 years to form, and they can range in color. These gems of the ocean are supposed to be healing for the heart, spleen, stomach, and intestines. Even though I am not a June baby, I was so excited when I was given pearl earrings, they are so beautiful and lustrous.

## 7. Ruby

![Ruby](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200750_7560_599x459.jpg)

The fiery red ruby of July represents devotion, integrity, courage, and happiness. It also stands for vitality, confidence, and strength. The deep red color is associated with fire, power, and importance. Rubies also enhance energy and generosity, and bring prosperity and success. Wearing rubies is supposed to increase positive thoughts and heal infections.

## 8. Peridot

![Peridot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200902_8137_599x459.jpg)

August’s birthstone, the peridot, is a lime-green gemstone that is the national gem of Egypt. This stunning birthstone represents love, truth, faithfulness, fame, dignity, protection, and loyalty. Peridots are another gemstone that are supposed to enhance prosperity, but they also encourage growth and openness. The peridot is great for problems with the lungs, lymph, sinuses, and breasts.

## 9. Sapphire

![Sapphire](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200923_3391_600x530.jpg)

The beautiful deep blue sapphire is September’s gemstone. This gemstone is said to have healing properties for fevers, inflammation, cancer, and burns. It is also supposed to banish evil thoughts. If you are a September baby, your birthstone stands for truth, sincerity, constancy, clairvoyance, insight, and interpretation.

## 10. Opal

![Opal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29200947_4365_599x459.jpg)

Representing hope, innocence, and purity, the opal is October’s birthstone. Opals are beautiful gemstones that have a white or black body with glints of other colors. They enhance visualization, imagination, dreams, and healing of all kinds. Opals are also said to give their wearer happiness, faithfulness, loyalty, and confidence.

## 11. Topaz

![Topaz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29201041_6069_599x311.jpg)

November’s birthstone, topaz, comes in many colors, but the orange-red color is considered to be the most valuable. These gemstones provide strength, balance emotions, enhance spiritual rejuvenation, increase happiness, and release tension. They also heal asthma, gout, blood disorders, and insomnia.

## 12. Turquoise

![Turquoise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/12/29201240_6659_599x570.jpg)

Turquoise is December’s gemstone, and it brings prosperity, success, happiness, and good fortune. Additionally, it encourages open communication, regeneration, strength, and protects against all diseases. Turquoise also enhances creativity, serenity, and spiritual bonding. The blue-green color of turquoise is absolutely beautiful, and it stands for the cycle of life.

Each of these birthstones has special properties that date back to ancient times. I think all of the meanings behind the birthstones are fascinating. However, if you are like me, you are partial to your own birthstone. What is your birthstone?
