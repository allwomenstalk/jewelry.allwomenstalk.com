## The Visionary Craftsmanship of Pomellato

Pomellato has a history of defying conventions, and the _Aquamarine Dream_ is a testament to that. Each link in the chain is meticulously crafted, moving like fluid waves in harmony with the body. The Maison's bold vision shines through the gold links, which seem alive, capturing the lively spirit of the Mediterranean itself.

## An Ocean in a Jewel

The cabochon aquamarine, weighing a remarkable 37.98 carats, is at the heart of this creation. Its smooth surface reflects light in a dance of blues, reminiscent of sunlight playing on the sea. Below it hangs a 15.95-carat pear-cut pendant, adding a touch of elegance and sophistication.

Each diamond, precisely set into the gold, enhances the necklace's grace. The gold itself, exquisitely polished, mirrors the intricate detailing of traditional Italian craftsmanship. With a price carefully positioned to match its beauty, this piece beckons to the soul, asking us to appreciate the world through a lens of exquisite detail and elegance.

## A Glimpse Behind the Scenes

What truly sets Pomellato apart is its dedication to craftsmanship. Behind the creation of the _Aquamarine Dream_ is an amalgamation of inspiration and innovation. The sketches, the trial pieces, and the final assembly-all of these steps combine to form a narrative, a journey from concept to creation.

Each piece is a conversation between artist and material. Pomellato's skilled artisans have turned aquamarine and gold into symbols of timeless beauty. A process of precision and patience brings each jewel to life, ensuring that every inch of the necklace exudes perfection.

## Embracing the Mediterranean Spirit

Wearing the _Aquamarine Dream_ connects one to the Mediterranean-a region rich in culture and history. It is an invitation to imagine sailing on clear waters, feeling the sun's warmth, and hearing the gentle splash of waves against the shore.

The necklace draws us back to nature while standing gracefully as a masterpiece in the art of jewelry design. Pomellato's inclusion of the diamonds, echoing the sparkle of stars dancing on midnight waves, enhances this dreamlike quality.
