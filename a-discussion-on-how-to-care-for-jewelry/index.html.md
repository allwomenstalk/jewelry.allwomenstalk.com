# 6 Excellent Tips on How to Care for Your Jewelry ...

Women (and men) have been wearing jewelry since the dawn of time. Adorning ourselves with jewelry has always helped to convey a certain personal style as well as showcased our status. Silver has always been one of the precious metals that was used for making those pieces. And both ladies and gentlemen wear silver jewelry today just as they have had for centuries.

Silver is used in making rings, anklets, bracelets, chains, and other luxurious items. It is very reflective and offers magnificent shine when cared for properly, that is why you need to know how to care for your precious pieces, especially if it's [designer silver jewelry](https://www.georgjensen.com/en-us/jewellery).

Taking care of silver jewelry does not come to only safe keeping, it's making sure it does not come in contact with harmful elements such as excessive heat and chemicals. Below we will take a look at how to take care of your jewelry, including how to give it a proper shine and prevent tarnishing.

**Tips on How to Maintain for Your Jewelry:**

## 1. Avoid Leaving Your Jewelry on Treated Surfaces

It is advised not to leave jewelry on wooden surfaces or chemically treated surfaces. This is because the sin in wood or the chemicals on the surface can act as a catalyst and thus increase the chances of the piece losing its shine.

## 2. Safe Storage

Proper jewelry storing is often disregarded. Jewelry should never be shoved into a drawer or on top of a dresser. This will subject them to scratches and other forms of damage.

Most jewelry pieces come in a box or a small flannel bag from the store, which is a perfect place to retain them. Sterling silver, for instance, should be kept in an anti-tarnish bag or cloth. Jewelry cases that feature separate padded slots for rings and posts for hanging necklaces and bracelets are also a great choice.

## 3. Keep Them from Chemicals

Exposure to compounds can damage or discolor precious metals, including Silver, Platinum, and Gold, and may as well harm some colored trinkets. Even day to day substances like lotion, hairspray, perfume, or other cosmetics can contain compounds that will permanently damage the exterior of your pearls and other subtle or absorbent gems like turquoise.

Fine jewelry should be removed before going into a chlorinated swimming pool or before using domestic cleaners. Many of these cleaners comprise ammonia, which can be a bit harsh for delicate gems or vintage jewelry. Chlorine bleach, another common domestic solvent, can damage gold alloys.

## 4. Keep Them from Light & Heat

Just as the sun's detrimental rays can damage our skin, heat and light can affect a colored gemstone's resilience and colour. Over time, and in surplus, they can also diminish or damage some gems, such as topaz and shell cameos. Pearls and other subtle materials, such as ivory, will whiten under extreme exposure to light. Other trinkets can darken over time when exposed to too much light.

Extreme heat and abrupt temperature changes may also break some gems. Heat can easily remove the natural dampness these gems needed to keep their beauty. Some, for instance, can dry out, crack and discolor. Others can turn white or brown, develop tiny cracks, and might lose their play-of-color.

## 5. Try an Ultrasonic Cleaner

If dirt and dust aren't removed easily from intricate pieces, then look into buying a cheap ultrasonic cleanser and follow the directives. Note that an ultrasonic cleaner won't remove tarnish, but it will work a treat with intricate pieces that have lots of crevices where dirt can amass and stick.

## 6. Use a Silver Dip

You can use a silver dip for rapidly and easily eliminating tarnish from Sterling Silver. It predominantly works well with silver chains, which can be problematic to clean with a polishing cloth alone. Silver dips are readily available in supermarkets, hardware outlets, or pharmacies. Follow the directives carefully and always rinse well and dry when you're done.

To sum it all up, you wear jewelry, but at the same time, you need to understand how to store it and clean it as we have seen above. These are expensive pieces, so let's take a good care of them.
