# 7 Great Necklaces by Monserat De Lucca ...

I love statement jewelry, especially statement necklaces, and one of my favorite brands is Monserat de Lucca by Defy. All of their best pieces are made of shiny brass, which develops a gorgeous vintage-feel patina with time. All of their necklaces are gorgeous, unique, and unusual, ideal for making a statement without uttering a word. Here are 7 great necklaces by Monserat de Lucca.
