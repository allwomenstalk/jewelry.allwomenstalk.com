# 7 Video Tutorials for DIY Collar Necklaces ...

DIY collar necklaces are the perfect way to save and look super trendy which, I’m sure you’ll agree, is a great reason to learn how to make a collar necklace all by yourself! DIY it, put your imagination into it and you’ll never feel torn between two equally great pieces or tempted by those fantastic yet quite pricey designer jewels. They are fun to do, a perfect project idea to consider next and a must-have no fashionista could say no to. Why wait, then? Take a look at these videos below and pick the DIY collar necklace tutorial you like the most.

## 1. Studded Collar Necklace

The best thing about DIY collar necklaces is the fact that you can decorate them anyway you like! And since we all love studs (right, girls?), I’ve decided to go with those first. Take notes from this quirky crafter right here and you’ll have your studded necklace in no time!

## 2. Pearl-Embellished Peter Pan Collar Necklace

If studs are a bit too fierce for your taste or you simply need an idea for your next DIY collar, give this trendy style a try! Oh and another pro – this DIY collar necklace tutorial makes it seem so easy! No sewing skills needed!

## 3. Pearls, Studs, Sparkles

Want a few more ideas on how to make a collar necklace? Well, here’s another fabulous take on studs and pearls, as well as a new shape and a new, sparkly style to check out! I love them all, although I find this black sparkly piece just a tad bit prettier than the others…maybe because it’s just my cup of tea?!

## 4. Chain Peter Pan Necklace

But here’s something for all of you girls who like to keep it simple! After all, who says a collar necklace can’t be a necklace all the way? Take a look at this interesting tutorial that explains how to make this amazing, beautifully simple design you’ll be able to wear with just anything!

## 5. Chain Collar Necklace

Let’s take a step in a different direction, shall we? You see, Peter Pan collars and necklaces are definitely not the only trendy accessory you can DIY at home. Originally posted as a fun DIY to help give you a few more ideas on how to wear a scarf, this video proves to be a fabulous source of ideas even if you’re hoping to DIY a necklace rather than a head cover!
