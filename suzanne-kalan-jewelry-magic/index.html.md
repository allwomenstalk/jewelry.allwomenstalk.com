## Why Emeralds?

Emeralds have been a symbol of rebirth and love for centuries. They are believed to grant the wearer foresight, good fortune, and youth, bringing a touch of magic and mystery to Suzanne Kalan’s designs. The choice of emeralds in her latest collection not only reflects a deep appreciation for ancient gemstone lore but also caters to the tastes of modern jewelry enthusiasts who crave authenticity and history in their collection. _Plus, who doesn’t need a sprinkle of extra luck in their daily life?_

Moreover, each ring from Suzanne Kalan is a testament to the brand’s dedication to ethical sourcing and sustainable jewelry-making practices. The emeralds and diamonds are carefully selected, ensuring that each purchase is not only beautiful but also responsible. Isn’t it comforting to know that behind every piece of jewelry is a commitment to the well-being of our planet?
