# 7 Jewelry Options for People with Metal Allergies ...

Don't let a metal allergy stop you from sporting fabulous jewelry. Guest contributor Rebecca Bastello shares some great choices that you turn to.

Having metal allergies can be a drag, especially when it comes to wearing jewelry. Every girl deserves the opportunity to wear stylish jewelry, even those who deal with rashes, green skin, swelling, and other symptoms of metal allergies. There are many fashionable alternatives to wearing metal jewelry that look and feel great. It’s easy to transform your look with any of these metal alternatives.

## 1. Leather

![Leather](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033648_4127_499x326.jpg)

Leather isn’t just for your accessories anymore. There are tons of great leather jewelry out there that will look and feel great on your skin. This leather wrap bracelet from Overstock is a trendy piece that will go wonderfully with any outfit. It’s also fashionable because it can be worn by both men and women.

## 2. Gemstones

![Gemstones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033651_4828_530x222.jpg)

Gemstones are highly sought-after for their brilliance, beauty, and various colors. A gemstone piece is a must-have for any jewelry lover out there, allergies or not. This Black Spinel Pave' Sterling Bar & Leather Bracelet from QVC is a luxurious combination of gemstone and leather that’s perfect for those suffering from metal allergies. The rich color bracelet is a showstopper that will take any look to new heights.

## 3. Glass

![Glass](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033652_7462_599x296.jpg)

For many, adding glass to their wardrobe would be a no-no, but if you’re looking for an alternative to metal jewelry, then get your hands on a glass piece. Glass jewelry is decadent and comes in many great designs that many won’t even realize is glass. This Bangles Set of 2 from Smart Glass Jewelry is a perfect foray into glass jewelry. The unique bangles come in a beautiful sage color and were designed from a wine bottle-now that’s amazing!

## 4. Hypoallergenic/Nickel Free Jewelry

![Hypoallergenic/Nickel Free Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033652_4067_599x271.jpg)

Hypoallergenic/nickel free jewelry might be the best alternative for those with metal allergies because you can easily get the great jewelry you love without the chance of an outbreak. Andy's Promise Ring from Emitations is great for the shopper who’s on the hunt for a commitment ring that has brilliance and shine that won’t upset your skin.

## 5. Seashells

![Seashells](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033823_0064_451x263.jpg)

Seashells are a stylish bohemian jewelry piece that looks great on necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and even rings. If you’re avoiding metal and are looking for something casual and chic, go for Isabel Marant’s Fuji Shell Bracelet from La Garconne. The slip-on bracelet features tiny seashells in a gorgeous marbled blue color. It’s an easy-going piece that’s perfect for summer looks.

## 6. Wood

![Wood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033648_4677_575x475.jpg)

Those with metal allergies, as well as eco-conscious consumers, are turning to wood jewelry to lower their impact on the environment. Wood offers a unique take on popular jewelry pieces like this Hamsa Bracelet from Good Wood NYC. Hamsa bracelets are trendy bracelets that are said to bring luck to those who wear them. The 100% maple wood bracelet is perfect for the fashionista who’s looking for a go-to-piece to wear with anything.

## 7. Thread

![Thread](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/16033845_3650_600x474.jpg)

Thread may seem more cheap than chic, but when laced with the right amount of embellishments can be quite fashionable. Tassel Macramé Bracelet by Cara from Nordstroms is a thrilling thread piece that makes a great everyday wear item that’ll add flair to your outfit.

You see, it’s easy and affordable to shop for jewelry that won’t cause an outbreak from metal related allergies. There are so many great designs and styles to choose from that make it painless to get excited about jewelry again. Ease your symptoms and enhance your look with any of these gorgeous jewelry options!
