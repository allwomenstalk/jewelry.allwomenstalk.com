# 10 of the Best Colourful Jewellery Buys ...

The best colourful jewellery can easily liven up an outfit. Lately, we’ve been noticing a trend for bright, bold, statement pieces. It could be an eye-catching pair of multicoloured earrings or an in-your-face neon necklace. Whatever you prefer, adding a splash of colourful jewellery to your outfit is a guaranteed way to stay on-trend. Check out the following selection of some of the best colourful jewellery buys right now.

## 1. Bright Bib Necklace

![Bright Bib Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/1_bright-bib-necklace_600x640.jpg)

A bright bib necklace is one of the best colourful jewellery buys I can think of right now. Team one with a crisp white shirt for a simple yet stylish look. This fun necklace will set you back around $60 from ASOS but you can find similar versions on the cheap at stores like Forever 21.

## 2. Colourful Swarovski Crystal Earrings

![Colourful Swarovski Crystal Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/2_colourful-swarovski-crystal-earrings_600x683.jpg)

Next time you’re accessorising your look, think about popping on some colourful statements earrings. These earrings are by Tom Binns, who is pretty much the master of all things colourful when it comes to jewellery right now. However, if you’re not ready to fork out $515 for a pair, check out By Malene Birger or ASOS for more affordable options.

## 3. Colourful Cuff

![Colourful Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/3_colourful-cuff_333x346.jpg)

Need to liven up that arm party with a bit of colour? Try adding a chic cuff with some colourful embellishments. This one is a steal at $10 from Modcloth, and you can check out more fun and colourful cuffs at Shopbop.

## 4. Beaded Necklace

![Beaded Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/4_beaded-necklace_328x400.jpg)

A heavily beaded necklace will really pack a punch. This one costs $50 at Modcloth and features an array of green beading. Work a luxe resort-inspired look and team a beaded necklace with a kaftan or maxi dress and metallic sandals.

## 5. Colour Burst Brooch

![Colour Burst Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/5_colour-burst-brooch_192x195.jpg)

A colourful brooch can be a cute addition to your outfit. Right now there are plenty of fresh and fun styles to choose from, like this colour burst brooch from Forever 21. At only $9, you’ll be tempted to stock up on more than one style of brooch!

## 6. Tassel Earrings

![Tassel Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/6_tassel-earrings_600x752.jpg)

If there’s one thing to add to your jewellery collection right now, it’s some statement making earrings. Tassel earrings have been around for a while now, and will probably be around for some time to come. These Oscar de la Renta earrings come in a brilliant turquoise hue. They’re pretty pricey though, so if you want to recreate the look on a budget, check out ASOS and BaubleBar.

## 7. Colourful Hinge Bracelet

![Colourful Hinge Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/7_colourful-hinge-bracelet_261x192.jpg)

Sometimes a single block colour is all you really need. A bold bracelet like this one will instantly liven up your look. While it only costs $9 at Forever 21, it does remind me of those luxury Hermès Clic-Clac bracelets.

## 8. Colourful Cocktail Ring

![Colourful Cocktail Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/8_colourful-cocktail-ring_240x240.jpg)

A colourful cocktail ring can be a great way to glam up your look. Choose one with some colourful gem details in your pick of colours. You can pick up a ring from designers like Kate Spade or stick to a budget with rings from Topshop and ASOS.

## 9. Geometric Necklace

![Geometric Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/9_geometric-necklace_328x400.jpg)

Geometric jewellery can really make a statement. Triangles are a popular shape right now and can be found on anything from earrings to bracelets. This necklace is a great example of how to work this trend on a budget. Costing only $5 at Forever 21, it would make a great addition to a plain outfit.

## 10. Flower Stud Earrings

![Flower Stud Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/01/10_flower-stud-earrings_600x527.jpg)

Daisy-like flower motifs are going to be big this year. Get a head start on the trend with these adorable flower stud earrings from ASOS. If you want to splash out, you can also try earrings from Marc by Marc Jacobs or Tiffany & Co.

Whether you’ve got deep pockets or are on a budget, there are plenty of stylish ways to work this season’s hottest colourful jewellery looks. Pop on a pair of earrings or slip on a colourful bangle to freshen up your outfit. How would you style these colourful jewellery pieces?
