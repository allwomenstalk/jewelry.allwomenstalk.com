# 15 Timeless  Pieces of Jewelry Every Lady Should Have ...

Every woman really needs to have a few classic pieces in her wardrobe (think LBD), but she also ought to have the timeless, "evergreen" jewelry pieces, too. What are these must-have bits of bling? Here's a handy list.

## 1. Strand of Pearls

![Strand of Pearls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o3/iu/llvcpxia5689cdfa1d2b6871410296_600x351.jpg)

Price: $450 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You can wear these with anything, from your fave LBD to your best interview outfit, and everything in between. Think Holly Golightly, and wear with panache.

## 2. Diamond Studs

![Diamond Studs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/bg/hbf4l1sc5689ce0caf53b073090879_600x154.jpg)

Price: $1,200 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I have a pair of diamond studs - granted, not this luxe - that never leave my ears. They're timeless and they truly go with everything.

## 3. Birthstone Jewelry

![Birthstone Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/nr/a3sds9y75689ce2275c30284888389_600x398.jpg)

Price: $4,550 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is an emerald bracelet, but whatever your birthstone, there's bound to be something glittery and glam with your own gemstone.

## 4. Fancy-Dress Watch

![Fancy-Dress Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c5/j6/ik7sioq95689ce325f44b526369119_600x488.jpg)

Price: $14,000 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Granted, it needn't be this dressy or spendy, but at some occasions, you won't want to drag out your iPhone to check the time.

## 5. Casual Watch

![Casual Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q9/kt/aa7v7kdw5689ce421f9f3983915067_600x213.jpg)

Price: $3,500 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
You'll also want a watch for every-day use, and this one would do nicely.

## 6. Statement Necklace

![Statement Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h4/xd/dls0ixsk5689ce53173e6757783329_600x400.jpg)

Price: $10,000 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This choker would make an excellent statement piece - it's elegant and classy, yet bold and daring.

## 7. Tennis Bracelet

![Tennis Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/a1/th2jjlwy5689ce7884678805403702_600x128.jpg)

Price: $19,000 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I wouldn't wear something this nice to play tennis, but ... I'd wear it everywhere else, for sure!

## 8. Charm Bracelet

![Charm Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vo/iw/a0zu9nyk5689ce882aad0306010090_600x402.jpg)

Price: $625 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whether you prefer a casual bracelet, like this one, or something you can personalize (like the ones from Ani and Alex), a charm bracelet is another classic piece.

## 9. Dainty Necklace

![Dainty Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jw/rt/mq2i5ki75689cefca538a370460955_600x558.jpg)

Price: $1,600 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
A dainty, delicate necklace is sort of the equivalent of diamond studs - versatile, something you can wear with anything, alone or layered.

## 10. Pearl Studs

![Pearl Studs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/ts/yv5xoi3h5689cf1c2e241007827990_600x136.jpg)

Price $225 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These delicate freshwater pearls make a statement with or without the matching strand of pearls.

## 11. Cuff Links

![Cuff Links](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/dq/fb3v4bmi5689ceacca5d4913438944_600x214.jpg)

Price: $475 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Cuff links aren't just for men! Most of us have at least one shirt with French cuffs, and while they do come with button-links, a pair of actual cuff links add so much style!

## 12. Beautiful Brooch

![Beautiful Brooch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w2/o3/u7lj07qp5689cebd50352750970089_600x304.jpg)

Price: $2,995 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whether you pin it to your coat or lapel, a brooch is an easy way to add color to any outfit.

## 13. Leather Bracelet

![Leather Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/jd/eyvg9em85689cedccaa67244304529_600x446.jpg)

Price: $350 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Whether you choose a thick cuff or a more dainty piece, a leather bracelet is classic, too.

## 14. Bangle Bracelet

![Bangle Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/l6/gkzsbeyn5689cde59cfa2099785922_600x196.jpg)

Price: $675 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Worn alone or in an arm-party stack, a bangle is timeless, too.

## 15. Your Own Signature Necklace

![Your Own Signature Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/gq/rl6b65tg5689ce65597fa281279168_600x462.jpg)

Price: $200 at [tiffany.com](http://www.tiffany.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This silver bean is my go-to necklace, my signature piece. When I don't wear it, people comment. Find a piece that matches your personality and that has meaning, and make it your own!
