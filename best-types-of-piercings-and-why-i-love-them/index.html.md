# 8 Best Types of Piercings and Why I Love Them ...

Have you ever been stuck in your conquest to find the best types of piercings for you? Well, I am going to give you some of my favorite types of piercings and why I think they’re so cool and will enhance your look. Piercings help express personality and give you some flair. I love them! So, here are my best types of piercings, I'm sure you'll find one you'd consider trying!

## 1. Nose

![Nose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/1_nose_600x900.jpg)

I love nose rings because they are so versatile. They can be hoops, studs, shape outlines, silver or gold, colorful or plain, or glittery rhinestones. I’ve had both the hoop and the stud, and I love the way they look. Nose piercings look great on almost anyone too, even celebrities like Ke$ha and Demi Lovato! Also, the great thing about it is that at some jobs, a small stud is totally appropriate! It's definitely one of the best types of piercings in my book.

## 2. Eyebrow

![Eyebrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/2_eyebrow_500x633.jpg)

I love eyebrow piercings. They give you a sexy, edgy, rocker look. They definitely add a cool dimension to any outfit too. They are versatile as well, because an eyebrow piercing can be spiked, a bar, or have a ball at the ends. I love that Fergie has one, and especially when Zoe Saldana rocked one in the film The Losers!

## 3. Septum

![Septum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/3_septum_536x800.jpg)

The septum piercing is becoming more and more popular these days, and I’m happy it is! This one is super versatile too. The ring can be worn many different ways! If you’re ever going for a job interview that is a bit more conservative style wise, you can always flip it up. Either way, this piercing is pretty awesome.

## 4. Monroe

![Monroe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/4_monroe_600x683.jpg)

This one oozes sex appeal. It pays homage the ever-quoted Marilyn Monroe, who was the ultimate sex symbol of her time. It adds a demure touch to your appearance. I love that it is elegant too! I would definitely love to try it one day!

## 5. Second Lobe

![Second Lobe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/5_second-lobe_500x546.jpg)

Most people already have their ears pierced, but why not try a second one? Having a second hole means more opportunities to have fabulous earrings! You have more room to get creative and can mix and match different hoops, dangles, studs etc. This is especially great for the more modest women. It looks great on everyone!

## 6. Helix

![Helix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/6_helix_600x899.jpg)

This one is cute and modest like the second lobe. But, this one is in an unexpected place! It turns up the style and looks great. I love that you can accentuate with jewelry at different parts on your ears with this type of piercing.

## 7. Navel

![Navel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/7_navel_500x747.jpg)

This piercing is another one that turns up the sex appeal. It looks great when you flaunt your tight tummy in your favorite bikini! It also looks great when wearing crop tops. It’s easily hidden if necessary too. They have so many different kinds, you could even find a ring with a cute charm!

## 8. Medusa

![Medusa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/07/8_medusa_600x900.jpg)

This look is so cool. I love it because it accentuates pouty lips perfectly. It’s right in the middle so it also brings symmetry to the face too. You’d be surprised at how symmetry attracts people!

I like piercings because they’re a unique way to express your personality on your body! It’s a great way to tell people who you are. But bear in mind, with piercings, you have to know how to properly clean and care for them regularly! Though there are other piercings I like, these are a very good snippet of my best types of piercings and the reasons why I love them so much. What are your favorite types of piercings? Are there any that I listed that you would try?
