Studded jewellery can add a touch of rock-chic glam to an outfit. From subtle embellishments to large studs, there are a variety of ways that you can incorporate the look into your wardrobe. Choose small stud earrings to wear during the day, and more obvious bracelets and necklaces for a casual off-duty look. Take a look at a few studded jewellery pieces below.
