# What Your Jewelry Says about You ...

When you put on your jewelry, you have chosen it because of the way to co-ordinates with your outfit or for the statement it makes. But did you know your choice also says things about you?

## 1. Bold, Colorful or Large

![Bold, Colorful or Large](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tu/oi/oykgisun5581ec40cc939894513343_399x601.jpg)

Or all three! When you go bold, what your jewelry says about you is that you’re not afraid to stand out. You want to be noticed, and you’re not afraid to be a show-stopper, and you have the confidence to handle all that attention with style. Big, bold, chunky pieces pair well with neutrals as well as bold colors, so you can tone it down or brighten up the look as much as you want. This daring jewelry adds zing to any outfit.

## 2. Personalized Jewelry

![Personalized Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/s9/m85uef0v5581efcde0931654907225_570x758.jpg)

Wearing personalized jewelry is a sure sign you’re the sentimental type. You wear your heart on your sleeve, as well as your neck, wrist, fingers, or any other place where you can wear jewelry. You’re not afraid of your feelings and you often express them freely and openly, even if you end up crying at a silly commercial or gushing at a drawing your children made. The personalized jewelry was probably given to you by someone who adores you.

## 3. Heavy Chains and Spikes

![Heavy Chains and Spikes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cy/yw/ha9ah2g45582868a25688758590182_600x902.jpg)

You are not one to follow the rules. In fact some would say you are a rebel. You want to stand out, but it’s because you want to be seen as a trendsetter and not restrained by convention. You’re edgy, full of adventure, and have no fear of crossing the line in both fashion and in life. You’re just as likely to mix your jewelry choices with leather as you are with lace, just so no one can quite predict your fashion moves.

## 4. Fine Jewelry

![Fine Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/pl/vhlyvy68558286cf16bf7263929919_500x665.jpg)

The woman who has several pieces of fine jewelry is announcing to the world that not only can she afford such finery, but she chooses quality over quantity. Fine jewelry can be anything from pieces made of gold or platinum to pieces encrusted with fine gemstones. They could even be pieces created by a well-known designer. If you wear fine jewelry, you avoid following the trends and choose pieces that will add a subtle accent to your wardrobe rather than competing with it. You choose timeless pieces that will serve you for years to come.

## 5. Antique or Vintage Jewelry

![Antique or Vintage Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/el/dl/cchzjt7r5582876ab670c328101213_590x830.jpg)

If you love scouring the antique stores and vintage fashion places for just the right jewelry, you’re probably very sentimental and refined. Even more, perhaps than the woman who prefers fine jewelry, you look for both quality and uniqueness. You love stumbling on that rare find that comprises both rare beauty and value. Women like you search for unique qualities and timeless beauty in your jewelry, your style, and everything else in your life.

## 6. Jewelry with Inspirational Messages

![Jewelry with Inspirational Messages](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pj/ye/oaps297j55828872840bb449472794_570x623.jpg)

Not only are you fashionable but you want your jewelry to be more than superficial decoration. Messages as serious as “I love you” inscribed on the inside of a ring to a bracelet emblazoned with the message that “love never fails” can provide a burst of inspiration throughout the day. If you wear this kind of jewelry, even if it’s just to remind you that it’s “not your circus, not your monkeys,” (inscribed inside your bracelet), you are a woman who likes efficiency and you don’t have time for reminders written on Post-It notes. This jewelry allows you to remain focused on the things that matter to you.

## 7. Delicate and Dainty Jewelry

![Delicate and Dainty Jewelry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j5/jx/feac9q1855828945cc400810442187_570x971.jpg)

Jewelry doesn’t wear this woman, she wears the jewelry. You are confident, you don’t feel the need to show off or be fussy about how you look. You love to have a few small accent pieces that go with everything. Sometimes you wear items that are sentimental, such as a necklace with your initial or a small locket with a picture of your children.

Do you think this is accurate to you? What are your favorite pieces of jewelry?
