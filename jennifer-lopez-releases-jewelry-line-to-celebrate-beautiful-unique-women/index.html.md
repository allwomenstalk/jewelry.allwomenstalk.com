# Jennifer Lopez Releases Jewelry Line to Celebrate Beautiful & Unique Women ...

If you're a fan of J.Lo's fashion-forward style, here's some amazing news... Jennifer Lopez has teamed up with Endless Jewelry to launch the Jennifer Lopez Collection! The customizable line of bracelets and charms will definitely find its way onto every girl's Christmas wish list.

The bracelets (ranging from $70 to $100) are crafted from genuine calf leather and are available in gold, black, or red with reptile finishes. They can be mixed, matched, and layered which makes decision-making that much easier because you can just opt for them all! As for the charms (every girl's favorite part), the line features a wide array of gold plated and sterling silver add-ons. You can choose between spiky studs, hearts, a "chain of love," and a whole lot more. The charms range from $45 to $145.
When speaking about the line, J.Lo said: “My exclusive collection with Endless Jewelry embraces the attitude of being fun and elegant at the same time. It is also about letting each woman express her personality through her jewelry. The collection will let women celebrate what is unique and beautiful about themselves!” Well, I'm sold. Are you?
