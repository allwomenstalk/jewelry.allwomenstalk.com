## A Touch Of Nature's Beauty on Your Finger...

Imagine the scene: vibrant sunflowers swaying softly in the gentle spring breeze. Now, imagine capturing that beauty in a piece of jewelry. That's what Harry Winston's Sunflower Collection seeks to achieve-a tribute to the elegance of nature itself. Each ring is designed to mimic the distinctive silhouette of a sunflower, with dazzling diamonds and colored stones forming the petals and center.

The sapphire-centered ring, for example, has a price tag of $12,000. It captures the deep blue of a clear spring sky, contrasting beautifully with the icy sparkle of surrounding diamonds. Meanwhile, the ruby-centered ring brings an intense pop of color, a bold statement at $11,500, perfect for the adventurous spirit who isn’t afraid to stand out.

## The Harry Winston Legacy...

Harry Winston has been a beacon of luxury since 1932, renowned for its commitment to unmatched craftsmanship and timeless design. This isn't the brand's first foray into nature-inspired collections. In fact, the Sunflower Collection follows in the illustrious footsteps of past series, each celebrating a different aspect of the natural world.

The magic lies in how Harry Winston manages to encapsulate the delicate beauty of nature in fine jewelry. By combining state-of-the-art techniques with classic design elements, they ensure each piece is not just a purchase, but an heirloom.
