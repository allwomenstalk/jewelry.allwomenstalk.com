# 7 Shortlived Bracelet Fads That Died out before We Even Realized ...

Just like any other fashion accessory, there have been tons of different bracelet fads throughout the years. The jewelry is popular one day, but seems to disappear the next. Here are some of my favorite bracelet fads that you’ll probably remember. Chances are you’ve even worn some of them in the past.

## 1. Sillybandz

![Sillybandz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/1_sillybandz_317x317.jpg)

One of the most recent bracelet fads was Sillybandz. These rubber bands came in different shapes and colors, and were aimed at children. Of course, everyone ended up buying them, despite their age. Some are in the shape of animals or cartoon characters, while others glow in the dark. Anything you can think of, there’s probably a Sillyband of it.

## 2. Livestrong

![Livestrong](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/2_livestrong_449x191.jpg)

Remember these? The Livestrong wristbands supported a good cause. The proceeds went toward treatment, emotional support, and insurance for cancer patients. There was a time when everyone sported these yellow bracelets. Now seeing them is rare, but they’re still available on livestrong.com/Shop.

## 3. I Love Boobies

![I Love Boobies](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/3_i-love-boobies_599x442.jpg)

Some people wore these bracelets because they thought it seemed silly, but the meaning couldn’t be more serious. This is another piece of jewelry that went toward a good cause. The money earned from this bracelet helped people learn about breast cancer prevention. They come in an array of different colors, but all have the same phrase across it. If you don’t have one, it’s never too late to buy it.

## 4. Jelly Bracelets

![Jelly Bracelets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/4_jelly-bracelets_600x517.jpg)

When I was in elementary school, everyone had an armful of these bracelets. Some used the different colors in order to indicate what kind of sexual activities they’d be willing to do, but most wore them innocently. They came in packs, so you never had to wear only one. You could even intertwine them so that you made two boring bracelets into one colorful one. Why not bring back the fad and start wearing them again?

## 5. Snap Bracelet

![Snap Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/5_snap-bracelet_292x241.jpg)

These bracelets weren’t the most fashionable, but we wore them anyway. The fabric that contained the spring inside came in different colors and designs. They’d snap right onto your wrist and made for a toy to bend when you weren’t wearing them. They were mostly popular in the late 80s and early 90s, but can still be purchased online. Why not relive your childhood and slap one on?

## 6. Friendship Bracelets

![Friendship Bracelets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/6_friendship-bracelets_600x431.jpg)

You still see these around today, but they’re not as popular as they once were. Friendship bracelets could contain as many beads, colors, and words that the wearer wanted. What could be better than sporting something of your own creation? It might not come out perfectly, but it’ll make you smile to look down at something that you made with your best friends. It doesn’t matter how old you are, it’s never too late to wear a token of your friendship.

## 7. Layard Bracelet

![Layard Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2013/08/7_layard-bracelet_534x318.jpg)

Some people would spend class time working on their lanyards. They’d be obsessed with creating as many bracelets and key chains as possible and would even sell them to their friends. There were dozens of different colors to choose from and you could use any combination that you wanted. It also gave you the chance to release your creative energy. Lanyards were not only a great fashion accessory, but a great way to kill time.

Do you remember any of these bracelet fads? Did you wear any of them? Or is there one that I forgot to mention?
