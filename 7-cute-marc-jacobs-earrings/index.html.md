# 7 Cute Marc Jacobs Earrings ...

By now you probably know I have a thing for Marc Jacobs jewelry and accessories, but can you blame me? His designs are fun, fresh, and colorful, and they work so well with my wardrobe! I especially love his earrings, which range from dainty to daring, and coordinate with his necklaces (which, by the way, I also adore). Here are 7 cute Marc Jacobs earrings…

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Miss Marc Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lg/gm/s5xgfnoo54c8f6045a22d966169633_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This half-inch enamel-over-brass earrings feature one of my favorite Marc Jacobs characters, Miss Marc. They exactly match a necklace, also featuring the smiling Miss Marc wearing her adorable red heart-shaped sunglasses. They’re a post back, so they’re for pierced ears only. Cute!

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Script Signature Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Script Signature Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sf/f9/mvwhswpa54c8f6050cab9373438959_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Available in gold-tone or silver-tone brass, these post earrings measure about a half-inch across and are, plainly, a cursive script of the Marc Jacobs signature, all in lower case. They’re bright and shiny and would be a subtle way to show your love of all things Marc Jacobs.

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Marc Logo Disc Stud Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Marc Logo Disc Stud Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2011/02/mj1_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These flat-disc earrings, made of palladium-plated brass and enamel, exactly match another Marc Jacobs necklace, and would be an ideal way to complete the look. They measure about a half-inch across and feature the Marc by Marc Jacobs signature all the way around. I love the cornflower blue color; it looks great with the gold-tone accents.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Daisy Cutout Stud Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Daisy Cutout Stud Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/ji/a6r9tg3354c8f605c91ff869468162_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $38.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Wouldn’t these be darling with a floral-print dress? Or for a little girl? They’re tiny daisies with cut-outs for the center and petals, available in gold-tone or silver-tone, so they can match all of your other accessories.

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs Anabella Stud Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Anabella Stud Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/al/g7/agxou8s654c8f606a050b667355437_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These elegant (but somehow punk-sass) post-back earrings are also available in gold-tone or silver-tone, and measure about a half-inch across. They’re made to look like soft, drapey bows, with a cross-hatch texture. I love these… they’re so versatile!

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Flight Marquis Leaf Earrings

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Flight Marquis Leaf Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hr/nh/mrp7klbv54c8f6079b186469354975_396x410.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I know! Finally, a pair of Marc Jacobs that aren’t posts! These are leaf-inspired dangle earrings, featuring a row of glittering crystals on the outside arc of each. They’re suspended on sterling silver French wires, and measure about two inches long. Wear these with an LBD, silver and black pumps, and a loose, messy updo… spectacular!

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Snow Bunting Donna Studs

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Snow Bunting Donna Studs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/wc/tgh3yo7a54c8f6085fef0465448499_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Aren’t these adorable? They would be perfect for wearing during the holidays. They’re tiny red crystal birdies, set in 10-karat gold-plated brass… I love their tiny beaks and eyes… they’re so small (a half-inch cross), but so detailed! Adorable!

These are only a handful of the earrings designed by Marc Jacobs, and I want them all! I especially love the tiny birds and the smiling Miss Marc posts… but which do you like best? And what would you wear them with?
