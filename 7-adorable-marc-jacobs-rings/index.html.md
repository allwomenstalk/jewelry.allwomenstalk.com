# 7 Adorable Marc Jacobs Rings ...

I’m sure by now you’ve probably noticed I have a thing for Marc Jacobs accessories. They’re so hip, so casual, so unique, I can’t help myself! And besides, I’m sure you’ll agree with me, and you’d probably like to see more of his fab designs, right? Keep reading, sweetie! Here are 7 adorable Marc Jacobs rings!

## 1. Marc by Marc Jacobs Signature Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Signature Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cm/c4/ccc93w2k54c8f7acaebd4415699615_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this ring! It’s simple and chic, just a plain black enamel band with the Marc by Marc Jacobs signature in raised gold-tone letters all the way around. If you like the ring, check out the matching bracelet, too!

## 2. Marc by Marc Jacobs Snow Bunting Donna Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Snow Bunting Donna Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/q6/pgaertzm54c8f7ad5df69808041121_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I adore this entire collection, for the sweet little birdie and the bright red, glittering colors. This ring in particular is gorgeous, featuring a thin band of gold-plated brass and a huge, sparkling center stone made of faceted glass. It’s made to fit across two fingers, an ideal statement ring!

## 3. Marc by Marc Jacobs Love Dove Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Love Dove Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/xw/mdxv1y8t54c8f7ae23744983718576_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $98.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
How very sweet! These two little doves are perched atop a glimmering band of gold-plated brass, streamlined and adorable. There’s a matching pendant and earrings, too, to complete the romantic look. Wear the set together, or one piece at a time… I’d wear them together on Valentine’s Day.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs Enamel Hare Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Enamel Hare Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zk/qc/f4sd41u654c8f7aedd786925817944_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $78.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This darling enamel bunny is from the Fantastical Take Collection, and he seems made for wearing with a vintage-inspired floral print dress and ankle booties, or with dark-wash skinny jeans and a lacy tunic. I love his antique finish, and how he lays across your hand, like he’s resting, or getting ready to leap for joy!

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs Classic Marc Logo Band Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Classic Marc Logo Band Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/su/fn/e2tlmw8y54c8f7afa114b604005736_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This ring looks very much like the first one on my list, with the same signature in raised letters, but rather than black enamel, this ring is made of sky blue enamel over hematite and instead of gold-tone, it’s finished with silver-tone. It’s stunning, but casual, ideal for everyday wear, to add a pop of fun spring color to any outfit.

## 6. Marc by Marc Jacobs Wise Owl Ring

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Wise Owl Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ij/l5/w5behyip54c8f7b05795f763358253_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $67.99 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Here’s another ring from the Fantastical Tale Collection, this one a clever little owl, made with glittering Czech crystals and rhodium-plated brass. I love his cross little eyebrow, giving him a slightly gruff expression… I’d wear this with my favorite LBD and glossy black peep-toes.

## 7. Marc by Marc Jacobs Bolt Color Block Bangle Bracelet

![Marc by Marc Jacobs Bolt Color Block Bangle Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/xf/fvwn8g3d54c8f7b13782a339560164_394x296.jpg)

**Price:** $68.00 at [saksfifthavenue.com](http://www.saksfifthavenue.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I know this isn’t a ring, but I had to include it anyway because it’s so pretty! It’s made of sleek resin in a rainbow of colors, ideal for wearing with everything in your spring wardrobe, from your favorite LBD to a pair of skinny jeans and a tunic. There are four bold color combinations to choose from, and I love them all!

Of all these cute Marc Jacobs rings, I love that little bunny… or maybe the owl… best. They’re both so cute… but then, I also adore that blue enamel ring, too… which of these adorable Marc Jacobs rings do you like best, and why? Please share!
