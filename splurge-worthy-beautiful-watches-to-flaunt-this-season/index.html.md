# 7 Splurge\-Worthy Beautiful Watches to Flaunt This Season ...

Being a girl who loves fashion, I am always on the hunt for beautiful watches. Because I love sharing my findings with my lovely readers, I've put together this list of the 7 hottest watches to flaunt this season. Yes, they may be a little on the pricey side… but trust me girls, they are so worth it. These beautiful watches will definitely be attention getters. They are all absolutely gorgeous!

## 1. Nixon "Kensington" in All Gold

![Nixon "Kensington" in All Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23154002_0637_299x374.jpg)

This is one of my favorites, and is a shoe-in for my list of beautiful watches to wear this season. Its classic, simple, yet very stylish look is great because it makes it so versatile. The lovely gold finish is my favorite, however, it does come in other colors, including all white, black, crystal, rose gold, and silver. Coming in at $175, this watch is worth every penny.

## 2. Michael Kors "Mini-Size Parker Multi-Function Watch" in Rose Gold

![Michael Kors "Mini-Size Parker Multi-Function Watch" in Rose Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23152719_6037_349x508.jpg)

Michael Kors is a fashion genius, and this watch is another one of my absolute favorites. Its smaller face isn't too bold or overpowering, and its lovely rose gold finish is gorgeous! This watch is also available in silver, and comes in at $250.

## 3. Michael Kors "White Mid-Sized Chronograph Watch" in White and Gold

![Michael Kors "White Mid-Sized Chronograph Watch" in White and Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23152719_8302_300x300.jpg)

I can safely say that I will be spoiling myself with this beauty. I'm super picky when it comes to white watches. I love the subtle gold detailing on this one, and it looks absolutely stunning when stacked with cute bracelets. It's also a great winter transition watch! Score this lovely piece for $225.

## 4. Marc by Marc Jacobs "Blade" in Gold

![Marc by Marc Jacobs "Blade" in Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23152719_4495_260x347.jpg)

Probably the most well-liked watch from Marc Jacob's Marc collection, there is this fabulous piece. I love gold watches in the fall, and this one is to die for. I just love the etched detail all around the face. Snag this beauty for $275.

## 5. Marc by Marc Jacobs "Amy Dinky Double Strap Watch" in Tan

![Marc by Marc Jacobs "Amy Dinky Double Strap Watch" in Tan](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23152719_1834_300x300.jpg)

This watch is for those of you who like watches with smaller faces. The cute gold double strap adds more flair to the watch, and blends in beautifully with some arm candy. Whether you wear it stacked, or on its own, this watch is adorable. It also comes in white and black, and is priced at $175.

## 6. Fossil "Georgia Three-Hand Bangle Watch" in Smoke and Rose

![Fossil "Georgia Three-Hand Bangle Watch" in Smoke and Rose](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23153642_6145_599x539.jpg)

This is a great watch because it is not too overpowering or chunky. The great smoke gray color and subtle rose gold detail makes it a great fall to winter transition watch too! It has a mid-sized face, lovely leather band, and comes in a variety of colors. Grab this one for $95, and you won't be disappointed.

## 7. Kate Spade "Carlyle Strap Watch" in Black and Gold

![Kate Spade "Carlyle Strap Watch" in Black and Gold](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/23152719_2051_265x353.jpg)

Last, but not least, we have this absolutely adorable watch from Kate Spade. How sweet is this watch? It has a patent leather strap, and the bow-shaped face is just too cute. It's a great one for those who might not like round-faced watches as much. Kate Spade has a ton of stylish and beautiful watches with different looks and faces- so feel free to browse! This one is available for $195 and comes in all kinds of different colors and materials.

Well, that does it for my list of beautiful watches to look at if you're thinking about spoiling yourself with a good, worthy watch. Do any of you own any of the ones I listed? How do you like them? What do you all think of the candidates?
