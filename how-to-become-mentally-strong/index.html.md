# Inspirational Advice to Supercharge Your Mental Strength ...

Want to know **how to become mentally strong**? We spend a lot of time getting in shape, trying for beautiful skin, eating clean and staying healthy. Most of us spend much less time on mental health. But everything begins with the brain. Maybe you don't know how to strengthen it. Well, you're in the right place. Here's **how to become mentally strong**.

## 1. BE a FRIEND of YOURSELF

Don't criticize yourself every time you do something wrong. Don't let negative emotions get the best of you. Get rid of habits that make you envious or depressed. Bad thoughts eat our mental health. If things don't work out the first time, try again. Get rid of the distractions. It is up to you.

## 2. READ BOOKS

Read real-life stories. Read everything from newspapers to journals to blogs. Reading makes you more experienced. You learn stories that build a strong mind. Try learning something new every week.

## 3. ASK QUESTIONS of YOURSELF

If faced with situations - bad or good - ask yourself powerful questions. What could I do to change the situation? What could I do to make the situation better? What small thing can I do to move forward? What is the positive side of this problem? Every situation has its negative and positive sides. Concentrate on the positive sides.

## 4. PLAY GAMES

To get in shape, you need exercise. There are exercises for the brain as well. Sudoku, chess, crossword or applications you could install on your phone. Exercise your brain daily. It increases problem-solving skills.

Don't let each day look like yesterday.
