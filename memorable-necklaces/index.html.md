# 8 Memorable Necklaces ...

While I don’t wear any myself, I do enjoy window-shopping statement necklaces. Whether they feature bright metals or eye-catching embellishments, many of the most memorable necklaces can be compared to works of art. Not all of these necklaces may be pleasing to the eye – they may just as easily be memorable for all the wrong reasons. Either way, take a look at a few memorable necklaces below.

## 1. Marni Paillette-Embellished Bib Necklace

![Marni Paillette-Embellished Bib Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/rr/kidpj15p54c9055fdd5c9458175769_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $420.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/DVqf38)
This bib-style necklace is made partly from recycled plastic paillettes. The multicoloured paillettes are secured to a wool felt bib necklace and self-tie ribbon. It’s an ingenious way of working recycled materials into a high-fashion design. However, I’d be keeping my eye out for any DIY versions and tutorials.

## 2. Asos Revive Statement Angular Brass Bib on Linked Chain

![Asos Revive Statement Angular Brass Bib on Linked Chain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/hj/a7ychggm54c905612dcb8006499339_350x432.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/6MZMCf)
The angular brass panels definitely make this one of the more memorable necklaces about. The large brass pieces are attached to a chunky linked chain with a hook and clasp fastening. Keep your outfit simple and wear this necklace against a black top or dress.

## 3. Danielle Scutt Teapot and Fan Necklace

![Danielle Scutt Teapot and Fan Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g7/vg/mid1z0ye54c90562285d3190295883_350x456.jpg)

**Price:** $90.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/LdxAvk)
There is a lot going on with this necklace. The design hardly seems wearable! It features matt gold-tone fans and teapots attached to a choker-style chain link necklace. If you look closely you’ll see that the chains feature woven hair. Very memorable, indeed.

## 4. Fallon ‘All Points West’ Choker

_ **Price:** $250.00 at barneys.com_
As far as memorable necklaces on this list go, this design is probably one of the simplest. This silver necklace features five filigree engraved spikes, studs, and faux black pearls They are attached to a fairly short link chain. Wear this necklace with a silk blouse and jeans for a memorable daytime look.

## 5. Lanvin Swarovski Insect Necklace

**Price:** $1975.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jb98VY)
How could you possibly forget that a giant insect is hanging from your neck? This Lanvin necklace features a larger than life insect adorned with Swarovski crystals and pearls. This necklace also has a silk-satin ribbon that ties at the back. At nearly $2000, this necklace is definitely a case of look, don’t touch.

## 6. Topshop Jumbo Multirow Necklace

![Topshop Jumbo Multirow Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xj/yf/urkwewlh54c90564537c9221723390_350x448.jpg)

**Price:** $80.00 a [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zCboSc)
The bright beaded layers on this necklace are bound to stick in your mind. It would certainly make a colourful addition to any outfit. This necklace features 18 layers of multicoloured resin tube beads. This would look great paired with a simple white tank top and denim shorts.

## 7. Forever21 Leatherette Tassel Necklace

![Forever21 Leatherette Tassel Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2011/05/resave1_291x500.jpg)

**Price:** $8.80 at [forever21.com](http://www.forever21.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This necklace would easily jazz up an evening outfit. This necklace consists of a thick curb chain, smaller hanging chains, and leatherette tassel trims. Pop this on over the top of a cocktail dress for something a bit different.

## 8. Dannijo Cota Oxidised Brass Swarovski Crystal Necklace

![Dannijo Cota Oxidised Brass Swarovski Crystal Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/ty/cbw68pus54c905654b555493930363_350x525.jpg)

**Price:** $795.00 a [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/Axof8B)
Tribal inspired pieces are going to be big this season. This one definitely comes under the category of memorable necklaces. It’s made from oxidised brass and features a Swarovski crystal embellishment. It would definitely add a bohemian touch to a red carpet look.

These necklaces may not be to everyone’s tastes. Whatever your preferences, there’s no doubt that they are quite memorable. Which of these memorable necklaces do you like best?
