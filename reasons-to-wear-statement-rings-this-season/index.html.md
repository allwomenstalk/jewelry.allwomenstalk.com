# 7 Reasons to Wear Statement Rings This Season ...

Statement rings are always the perfect accessories to add to any outfit, no matter the season! But I particularly love them for spring, and I’m going to give you 7 reasons why! It took me awhile to really like wearing statement rings, because I was always more into dainty jewelry. But once I tried it a few times, I really loved wearing statement rings. Here are my top 7 reasons to wear statement rings this season!

## 1. Statement Rings Switch up Your Look

If you wear statement rings, then you know how easily they can help switch up your look! You can go from day to night, boho to edgy, or casual to party-ready with the right statement rings! Paired with the right mixture of bracelets, statement rings are easily the perfect piece of jewelry to complete any look. Or, you can wear them alone for a simple style!

## 2. Bring out Your Nails

Statement rings obviously draw attention to your hands, so your nails will be in the spotlight. I love to wear bright and bold or dark and dramatic colors on my nails when I wear statement rings! If your nails are chipped, make sure and touch them up before wearing a statement ring, because they will be noticed now more than ever before.

## 3. Statement Rings Speak for Themselves

One good thing about wearing statement rings is that you don’t need much more to complete your look. A simple jeans and t-shirt can be given some extra pizzazz if you throw on a statement ring or two! Big, bold rings are perfect for adding a pop of color or personality to an otherwise simple outfit. So if you’re in a rush, or don’t feel like trying one morning, you can still look pulled together with a few statement rings!

## 4. Easy Mixing and Matching

If you are the bold type, then you can understand this reason to wear statement rings! Statement rings are generally pretty easy to mix and match! You can wear silver and gold together, or other contrasting colors. Contrast your outfit with the rings, or contrast the rings with each other for a playful vibe. Don’t be afraid to mix textures and sizes with each other. Flat statement rings are as cute as cocktail statement rings!

## 5. One is Enough…or Not

With statement rings, you have lots of room to play around. Depending on your personal tastes and styles, you can play it safe and uncomplicated with just one ring. Trust me, you will still be noticed! But if you like to have fun and be the life of the party, play it a little more flirty and daring and throw on three or four statement rings! Just balance the look out with minimal or no jewelry elsewhere so you don’t look overdone.

## 6. Great Conversation Starters

I’ve done a lot of traveling to different countries and areas, so I’ve acquired some interesting pieces over time. I love to find the most unique rings, because they are great conversation starters, and each piece has its own story! If you love to stand out from the crowd, look for statement rings that don’t look like the ones everybody is wearing these days.

## 7. Effortless Style

Probably the best thing about statement rings is how easily they lend effortless style to any look. Celebrities wear them for practically any occasion, every magazine has at least one or two featured on models among its pages, and you can find them easily in any fashion store. Statement rings are pretty much a classic piece of jewelry to own and wear these days!

So, do you think you will rock any statement rings this season? If you’ve never tried it before, I would encourage you to at least give it a go. Statement jewelry of any kind can be intimidating, but if you start out slow and choose something not quite so bold, you will be well on your way to successfully styling statement rings this season!
