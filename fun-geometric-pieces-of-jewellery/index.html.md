# 7 Fun Geometric Pieces of Jewellery ...

**Geometric jewelry is a fun** way to make a bold statement. This season, we’re seeing geometric shapes adorn a range of items from clothes to bags to jewelry. I’d have to say that wearing geometric pieces of jewelry has to be one of the easiest and more versatile ways to wear this trend. Take some inspiration from the trendy geometric jewelry below.

## 1. ASOS Geomtric Splash Earrings

![ASOS Geomtric Splash Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/1_asos-geomtric-splash-earrings_500x501.jpg)

**Price:** $14.32 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/n7rXH6)
Novelty fashion items are trending this spring-summer season and these **geometric pieces of jewelry are the perfect** example. They are very cartoon-like in their design and come in a shocking pink colour. They’re made from plastic and have a sharp geometric hoop design. These oversized earrings would work really well to spice up a plain outfit such as denim shorts and a sweater.

## 2. Topshop Thread Cube Pendant

![Topshop Thread Cube Pendant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/2_topshop-thread-cube-pendant_278x560.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/zx1LvP)
This thread cube pendant would make a cute little detail to your outfit. It has a gold-look metal chain and accents, cube pendant, and thread-wrap details. While it’s been all about the collar necklace recently, drop pendant necklaces are making a comeback and this one has a lengthy 45cm drop. Wear this necklace with a white shift dress and other pastel accents for a sweet spring-summer look.

## 3. Oscar De La Renta Geometric Link Necklace

![Oscar De La Renta Geometric Link Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/3_oscar-de-la-renta-geometric-link-necklace_373x560.jpg)

**Price:** $475.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/jxZbSg)
If you want your **geometric jewelry to be** a bit more classic, stick to gold or silver finishes. This necklace has an intricate geometric link design. The range of shapes and angles make for some graphic jewelry. Wear it with a simple cocktail dress and heels for an instant outfit update.

## 4. ASOS Zig Zag Enamel Triangle Earrings

![ASOS Zig Zag Enamel Triangle Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/4_asos-zig-zag-enamel-triangle-earrings_500x566.jpg)

**Price:** $17.91 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/gm1SxK).
These black and gold earrings have a bit of a retro vibe about them. They have a triangle design with black zig zag patterns throughout. They have an oversized design so are not for the faint hearted. Wear these with a black maxi dress for an exercise in after hours dressing, or with a white silk blouse and denim flares for a retro daytime look.

## 5. Topshop Pyramid 3D Cage Ring

![Topshop Pyramid 3D Cage Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/5_topshop-pyramid-3d-cage-ring_500x495.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/wOfg06)
This cage ring is a more subtle way to wear the trend. The gold-tone finish is quite stylish, but the raised pyramids give it that geometric edge. Wear it alone has a statement piece of **geometric jewelry or incorporated** into a handful of rings.

## 6. Disaya Pink Macaroon Bracelet

![Disaya Pink Macaroon Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/6_disaya-pink-macaroon-bracelet_500x309.jpg)

**Price:** $205.91 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/6VKcIB)
This sweet bracelet features an assortment of circular pastel pendants. The four pendants are set in a gold-tone brass bracelet. The bracelet itself has a chunky chain link and is secured with a cute candy shaped fastening. This fun piece of jewelry would add a quirky touch to any outfit.

## 7. ASOS Coloured Cube Necklace

![ASOS Coloured Cube Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/03/7_asos-coloured-cube-necklace_360x560.jpg)

**Price:** $10.74 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/Xkqm75)
Nothing says ‘fun’ more than coloured jewelry. It’s also the case when it comes to geometric jewelry. Perfect for wearing day-to-day, this necklace has a slim gold-tone chain with five coloured cube pendants at the drop. For a fun weekend look, wear it against a complementary yellow, pink, or blue top.

When **it comes to geometric jewelry**, it’s all about choosing a piece that you find eye-catching. Bold novelty items are great for making a statement while a classic metal piece is easier to incorporate into your everyday wardrobe. What do you think of these geometric pieces of jewelry?
