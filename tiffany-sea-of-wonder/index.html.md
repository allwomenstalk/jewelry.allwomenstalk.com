# Discover the Secrets Behind Tiffany & Co.s Seahorse Design...

When you think of **Tiffany & Co.**, an image of timeless elegance and innovation likely floats to the forefront of your mind. Known for [intricate craftsmanship](https://www.instagram.com/tiffanyandco), this revered brand continues to captivate with their latest stunning collection-_The Seahorse chapter of Blue Book 2025: Sea of Wonder_. It's a journey beneath the waves, transforming the elusive beauty of the ocean’s creatures into opulent pieces that beguile and inspire.

Imagine being swept away by the rhythmic dance of the sea, enveloped in hues of deep sapphire blue, and glistening under the sunlight. That’s precisely the sensation evoked by the Seahorse chapter. These pieces aren’t just jewelry; they are _works of art_ that breathe life into your wardrobe-a modern homage to an original 1968 design. **Tiffany & Co.** weaves over 12 total carats of blue zircons, 5 total carats of moonstone, and a cascade of sapphires into their enchanting brooch and rings, marrying the allure of the ocean with sophisticated luxury.

For those who are mesmerized by both the sea and exquisite craftsmanship, this collection is nothing short of a dream materialized in precious gems. Every piece echoes the serene yet powerful essence of the sea, a masterpiece ready to be adored by the sophisticated collector.

## The Legacy of Seahorse Design

The seahorse is a motif deeply rooted in **Tiffany & Co.’s** legacy, symbolizing strength and mystery. The brand’s newest venture into this realm brings forth a version that sparkles with over 17 total carats of blue zircons surrounded by brilliant diamonds and sapphires. When you wear these rings, you carry a piece of the ocean, eternalized in sapphire and silver.

### Blue Zircons: Ocean’s Heart

Zircons are known for their diamond-like brilliance and a multitude of colors, particularly the captivating blue. These gemstones, central to the collection, shimmer like the ocean on a bright sunny day. Tiffany’s skilled artisans have lovingly cut and framed each zircons, ensuring that every angle dances with light.

**Blue zircon ring:** $15,000

**Seahorse brooch:** approximately $25,000

## Moonstones and Sapphires: Celestial and Oceanic Interplay

Enhancing the natural beauty of zircons in the collection are _cabochon moonstones_, which add an ethereal glow akin to moonlight reflected on the water. These moonstones are specifically chosen and crafted to provide an uninterrupted play of light across their smooth surfaces, making them a gentle contrasting companion to the sharp brilliance of sapphires.

Imagine slipping on a ring where the cool depth of sapphire is both framed and underscored by the soft luminescence of moonstone. It’s not just a piece of jewelry; it’s a statement of poise and passion for the ocean’s depths.

**Moonstone and sapphire ring:** $18,500
