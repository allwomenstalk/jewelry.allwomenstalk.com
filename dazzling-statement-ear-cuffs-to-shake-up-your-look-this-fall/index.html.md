# 7 Dazzling Statement Ear Cuffs to Shake up Your Look This Fall ...

Statement ear cuffs are a great way to shake up your look for fall. Ear cuffs are certainly having a trending moment right now and they come in a range of different shapes, sizes and designs. Whether you’re after a different take on cocktail jewellery or a stand-out daytime accessory, an ear cuff is the right way to go about it this season. Take a look at these statement ear cuffs that will add that extra spark to your outfit.

## 1. Swarovski Crystal Ear Cuff

![Swarovski Crystal Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040615_9949_599x744.jpg)

Ryan Storer is gaining quite a cult following thanks to his dazzling statement ear cuffs. Featuring an array of Swarovski crystals, his ear cuffs are the ultimate statement makers. This rose-gold ear cuff features faceted Swarovski crystals, is inlaid with four pearls, and has a double closure for non pierced ears. It also comes with a single stud earring inlaid in rose gold. Be prepared to splash out though, as this ear cuff will set you back a couple of hundred dollars.

## 2. Jewel Ear Cuff

![Jewel Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040614_2806_599x612.jpg)

For a more budget-friendly affair, you can’t beat ASOS for on-trend pieces of jewellery. This jewel embellished cuff costs around $30 and would easily add some spark to your favourite party outfits. It’s set on a slim gold-tone hook and the entire cuff just hooks on to the ear. Try pinning your hair back on one side to really show it off.

## 3. Metal Stone Ear Cuff

![Metal Stone Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040616_0426_599x629.jpg)

This ear cuff has more of an industrial feel to it. It’s by BCBGMAXAZRIA will set you back around $38. It features rectangular metal stone embellishments on a wire ear cuff. This gold-tone ear cuff would look great worn during the day with a basic outfit of a chambray shirt and relaxed-fit shorts.

## 4. Jewelled Ear Cuff Two Piece

![Jewelled Ear Cuff Two Piece](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040616_4513_599x392.jpg)

The most popular look is to wear one statement ear cuff at a time, but if you prefer a bit more symmetry in your day then pick up a pack of two ear cuffs to wear instead. These jewelled ear cuffs are from ASOS and are priced at around $35. They feature mixed blue jewels in a floral-inspired design. They clip on to the ear lobe and upper ear. Team them with a crisp white shift dress and metallic flats for a simply chic outfit.

## 5. Ice Crystal Ear Cuff

![Ice Crystal Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040646_4531_340x197.jpg)

This delicate pastel crystal ear cuff has a bit of a vintage-inspired flavour to it. It’s made from gold-tone metal and features assorted crystals laid out in a leaf design. This ear cuff would look great teamed with flowing dresses and plenty of pale shades. You can grab this ear cuff online from Nasty Gal for under $20.

## 6. Triangle Ear Cuff

![Triangle Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040614_3814_599x612.jpg)

This ear cuff will have you seeing triangles! It’s made from silver-tone metal and features pave stone embellishments set in an edgy geometric design. It also features an adjustable cuff at the top and stud closure to bottom. This stunning piece of statement jewellery costs around $30 from ASOS. For fall, team it with a chunky black knit, dark denim jeans, and patent shoes for a cool off-duty look.

## 7. Five Ring Ear Cuff

![Five Ring Ear Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/08/30040712_2972_600x574.jpg)

For something a bit more understated, try this sleek five ring ear cuff from Forever 21. It doesn’t feature jewels or embellishments but the high polish gold-tone metal still packs a punch. The best part is that it will only set you back around $4. Wear it with your skinniest jeans and a leather jacket for a rock chic inspired look.

These are just a few statement ear cuffs that will definitely add a bit of spark to your outfits this fall. What do you think of the ear cuff trend? Which of these ear cuffs would you wear and how would you style it?
