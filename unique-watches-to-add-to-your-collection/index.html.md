# 7 Unique Watches to Add to Your Collection ...

Do you like to collect unique watches? Are you looking to expand your collection? If so, this article is just for you! Included in this post are seven watches that are fun, different, and functional. Watches have become an important accessory, and some can be considered as jewelry. So here are seven unique watches to add to your collection.

## 1. ModCloth’s Time Flies by Watch

![ModCloth’s Time Flies by Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/05/tc/vnz9w1dx_600x857.jpg)

ModCloth has a lot of unique watches. The Time Flies By Watch, in particular, is really different. The face of this watch has a blue butterfly on it. It has a dark brown leather band and gold buckle and trim. I think this watch would look great with some gold and multi-coloured bracelets.

## 2. ModCloth’s Wood You Have the Time? Watch

![ModCloth’s Wood You Have the Time? Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/el/uhkkk8hp_600x857.jpg)

This watch is perfect for those who like the natural look. ModCloth’s Wood You Have the Time? Watch is comprised of 100% natural wood. Cool, right? This item contains silver accents and roman numerals. What’s really great about this watch is that WeWood (the company of the watch) will plant a tree for every watch that is sold.

## 3. ModCloth’s Wrist from a Rose Watch

![ModCloth’s Wrist from a Rose Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yl/u1/dmym77g0_449x576.jpg)

ModCloth’s Wrist From a Rose Watch is also very unique in the sense that it’s completely pink. The hands, trim, band, and face of the watch is pink. Made of rose-gold, this item is perfect to bring some colour to a dull outfit. What’s also unique about this watch is that the band is really long, which means that you can wrap it around your wrist twice. So basically you are gaining a watch and a bracelet with this item.

## 4. ModCloth’s Elegant in Any Occasion Watch

![ModCloth’s Elegant in Any Occasion Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q2/us/ubq0t5oa_600x857.jpg)

Like its name suggests, ModCloth’s Elegant in Any Occasion Watch is truly elegant. While the overall look of this watch is rather traditional, it also contains characteristics that are unique. For example, the band of this watch is pink leather. The pink really gives this item a feminine touch. The face of the watch is relatively plain, with Roman numerals. I think this watch has a 80's feel to it. What do you think?

## 5. Target’s Woman’s Xhilaration Love Strap Watch

![Target’s Woman’s Xhilaration Love Strap Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/e6/r1vlshcu_410x400.jpg)

This watch is really unique, isn’t it? Target’s Woman’s Xhilaration Love Strap Watch is brown, gold, and designed with the word love across the band. I’ve never seen a watch like this before. Have you? I think this watch could be considered jewelry.

## 6. ModCloth’s Wrap Time Watch in Black

![ModCloth’s Wrap Time Watch in Black](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i0/ta/z5tw07md_600x840.jpg)

I love how this item is both a watch and a bracelet. ModCloth’s Wrap Time Watch in Black is comprised of a long black leather strap which you can wrap around your wrist three times. I think this watch is both functional and fashionable. What do you think?

## 7. Target’s Women’s Xhilaration Elephant Dial Analog Watch

![Target’s Women’s Xhilaration Elephant Dial Analog Watch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m1/mi/jiesw1e4_410x408.jpg)

Do you love elephants? If so, Target’s Women’s Xhilaration Elephant Dial Analog Watch is just for you! The face of this watch has tiny black elephants printed on a blue background. Cute, right? This item has an alloy metal band with a bejeweled face trim. I think the elephants and the bejeweled trim really add a unique touch to the watch.

That was seven unique watches. Would you add any of these watches to your collection? Which watch do you think is the most fashionable and unique? Which watch is your favourite from this list?
