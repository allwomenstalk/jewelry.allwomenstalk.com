# 8 Gorgeous Tiffany Celebration Rings ...

Whether you’re celebrating the birth of a child or your wedding anniversary, one marvelous way to commemorate the event would be a celebration ring, a glittering reminder of that special day. Naturally, if you’re going to be buying (or receiving) a celebration ring, or any other fine jewelry, the first place to shop would be Tiffany. They offer the most beautiful rings, and are sure to be treasured for generations to come. Here are 8 gorgeous Tiffany celebration rings.

## 1. Tiffany Garden Cobblestone Band Ring

_ **Price:** $9,900.00 at tiffany.com_
Inspired by cobblestone garden path, this gorgeous celebration ring is circled all the way ‘round with more than 2 total carats of rose and round cut diamonds. At the top and bottom, the path is bordered by two even rows of diamonds, giving the ring a stunning definition around its careful tranquil chaos.

## 2. Tiffany Channel-Set Band Ring

_ **Price:** $3,800.00 at tiffany.com_
More than a full carat each of brilliant round diamonds and deep blue sapphires circle this 3 millimeter platinum band, all channel set and glittering. The stones are set all the way around the band, making this a true eternity ring. It’s available in whole and half sizes from 4 to 8, and would make a gorgeous wedding or anniversary band.

## 3. Tiffany Etoile Band Ring

_ **Price:** $1,900.00 at tiffany.com_
Around each of these round-cut diamonds is a thin ring of platinum, all set together in a gorgeous 18-karat yellow gold band. It’s a very modern-looking band, and whimsical, for the uneven setting of the stones.

## 4. Tiffany Aria Ring

_ **Price:** $12,500.00 at tiffany.com_
More than two and a half carats of glittering round diamonds, all grouped together in a stunning triad pattern, circle this celebration band of pure platinum. Since it’s such an unusual shape, not a classic band, it’s ideal for wearing on its own, and it’s so eye-catching!

## 5. Tiffany Swing Ring

_ **Price:** $3,850.00 at tiffany.com_
What an elegant, feminine ring! It’s a platinum band featuring more than a half-carat of brilliant round-cut diamonds in ones and twos. It would look stunning stacked or just by itself… it’s dainty and pretty and would be such a lovely anniversary band or mother’s ring!

## 6. Tiffany Jazz Graduated Band Ring

_ **Price:** $2,600.00 at tiffany.com_
Though technically this isn’t an eternity ring, the lush pink sapphires and diamonds do circle half-way around the platinum band, all bezel-set and round-cut, alternating between pure gleaming white and brilliant petal pink.

## 7. Tiffany Etoile Four-Row Band Ring

_ **Price:** $11,950.00 at tiffany.com_
This is the ultimate in glittering and shine, the ideal way to celebrate something special! It’s four rows of pavé-set round diamonds, all set in gleaming platinum. It’s sure to be noticed, with nearly three carats in diamonds, and would be the perfect way to commemorate an anniversary or another special event!

With so many beautiful celebration rings to choose from, and so many special events to celebrate and commemorate, the only question is, which is the best for each occasion? I adore them all, especially the first one… which of these Tiffany celebration rings do you like best, and what would you celebrate with it? Do tell!
