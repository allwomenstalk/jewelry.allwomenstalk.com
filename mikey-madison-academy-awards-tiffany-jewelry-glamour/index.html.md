# Glamour in the Spotlight: Mikey Madison's Academy Awards Preparation

As the lights dim and anticipation fills the air, the Academy Awards® is a pinnacle event in Hollywood. One of the stars getting ready for this unforgettable night is Mikey Madison, a dazzling ambassador for House Tiffany & Co. The elegance of her choice in jewelry showcases a beautiful blend of historical artistry and contemporary flair.

## The Perfect Jewelry Selection

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/3baf68b7-325f-45b0-925d-05908727bfd3.jpg)

For the occasion, Mikey shines in a stunning necklace sourced from The Tiffany Archives, dating back between 1909 and 1919. This exquisite piece is crafted in platinum-iridium and features intricate diamond embellishments that truly stand out against her classic black attire. Complementing this masterpiece is a luxurious Tiffany High Jewelry bracelet, also in platinum, adorned with over 17 carats of sparkling diamonds.

## Attending in Style

![image](https://img.allw.mn/uploads/75984b18-e5bf-444e-8cb3-66295dac75a9.jpg)

No glamorous look is complete without the perfect rings, and Mikey doesn't disappoint. She chooses to accessorize with a striking ring set in platinum, featuring over 9 carats of diamonds, and another ring showcasing a beautiful pink tourmaline of over 6 carats. The thoughtful combination of colors and materials not only elevates her look but also pays homage to the legacy of Tiffany & Co.

The glamour of Hollywood intertwines with the artistry of jewelry design, and in the case of Mikey Madison, it results in nothing short of breathtaking. Each piece tells a story, making her a true standout on one of the biggest nights in film.
