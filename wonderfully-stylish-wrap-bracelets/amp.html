8 Wonderfully Stylish Wrap Bracelets ...

John Hardy Dragon Wrap Bracelet • Kitson Double Strand Wrap Bracelet • Pieces Sava Wrap Bracelet • ASOS Wrap Wristband • Forever 21 Beaded Wrap Bracelet • More ...

By Sophia

Wrap bracelets make for great pieces of casual jewellery. From colourful braided fabrics to sleek black leather, the wrap bracelet comes in many forms. The main appeal of the wrap around bracelet, to me, is that it can look like you’re wearing multiple bracelets. So effectively, you’re getting more bang for your buck! See what you think of the wrap bracelet from the examples below.

1. John Hardy Dragon Wrap Bracelet

Price: $325.00 at net-a-porter.com
Animal motifs are always a popular choice when it comes to jewellery, and the focal point of this wrap bracelet is a sterling silver dragon. Combined with the braided black leather, this bracelet would make a sleek addition to any outfit.

2. Kitson Double Strand Wrap Bracelet

Price: $12.53 at us.asos.com
This bracelet has a wonderfully dainty design. It features a row of diamante crystals set in a gold-tone metal trim. It also has an adjustable knotted closure. The white colour is bright and refreshing, making it perfect for teaming with a colourful summery outfit.

3. Pieces Sava Wrap Bracelet

Price: $25.07 at us.asos.com
This wrap around cord bracelet is a striking piece of casual jewellery. It has a woven multi strand design with a metal keeper at the centre. This bracelet is available in both blue and black colours. The blue would look great as a funky addition to your look. However, basic black would be much more versatile.

4. ASOS Wrap Wristband

Price: $7.16 at us.asos.com.
Brightly coloured jewellery is on-trend for the season. This yellow bracelet has a layered cotton main and bead fastening at the rear. If you’re heading to any festivals this upcoming summer, this wrap bracelet would be a fun way to accessorise your festival look. Wear it alone or with an array of other bracelets for a decoratively layered arm.

5. Forever 21 Beaded Wrap Bracelet

Price: $7.80 at forever21.com
Add some glamour to your look with this striking gold-effect bracelet. It has an assorted bead pattern and has a stretchy body. This is one of those wrap around bracelets that will make people think you’re wearing multiples.

6. ASOS Leather Wrap around Bracelet

Price: $14.32 at us.asos.com
This bright yellow bracelet is a fun way to wear this trend. It’s made from leather and has an adjustable buckle fastening. This bracelet would look great worn with this season’s surf trend. Team it with an equally bright outfit and accessories.

7. Alexander McQueen Skull Charm Wrap Bracelet

Price: $325.00 at net-a-porter.com
If it’s a designer touch your after, then is Alexander McQueen bracelet should be right up your alley. It features the designer’s iconic skull detailing as well as a brass disc charm. The leather bracelet itself has a taupe watersnake finish. Wear this bracelet with a white blouse and blue jeans for a simple, off-duty look.

8. Chan Luu Wrap Bracelet

Price: $325.00 at net-a-porter.com
This wrap bracelet channels the tribal trend that is so popular right now. It features multicoloured cotton cord with rhodium plated nuggets. This bracelet is the one of the easiest ways to give off the effect of wearing stacked bracelets.

Wrap bracelets are your ticket to new season cool. When wearing bracelets this season, make sure you have at least one wrap around style in your arsenal. Which one of these wrap around bracelets do you like best?
