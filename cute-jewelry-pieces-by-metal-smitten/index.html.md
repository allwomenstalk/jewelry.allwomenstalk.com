# 7 Cute Jewelry Pieces by Metal Smitten ...

Years ago, I had to prove to my daughter just how old I was by asking her to explain what, exactly, “less than three” meant. She rolled her eyes and told me to tilt my head to the right… it’s a heart! Now I love the symbol, and use it all the time… so I was tickled the first time I saw the “less than three” jewelry by Metal Smitten, one of my favorite indie jewelry-makers. If you’re with me, and infatuated with this texty symbol of love, keep reading! Here are 7 cute jewelry pieces by Metal Smitten, most with that darling, sweet sentiment.

## 1. <3 Heart Charm Bracelet

_ **Price:** $40.00 at shanalogic.com_
The same sweet message that you’ve seen on all of the other pieces, now suspended from a gorgeous sterling silver charm bracelet! What a wonderful gift, don’t you think? It’s 7 inches long, with a sturdy lobster-claw clasp.

## 2. <3 Heart Ring

**
_ Price:** $55.00 at shanalogic.com_
Declare your love for your own sweetheart with this dainty little band, custom-made of sterling silver in size 4 to 12, it is stamped with the text-chic “less than three” symbol. It’s thicker than most little rings, and so comfortable! Stack it or wear it alone, either way, it’s darling!

## 3. <3 Heart Dangle Earrings

![<3 Heart Dangle Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2011/06/earrings_300x191.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [etsy.com](http://www.etsy.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These 10-millimeter sterling silver discs hang from a French hook, and each is stamped with the “less than three” I’ve come to love using as a signature in all of my text messages and emails. They hang about one inch long, so be sure to wear your hair up in a loose ponytail or bun so everyone can see them… and admire them! After all, the best part about wearing a cute jewelry piece by Metal Smitten is showing it off!

## 4. <3 Heart Stud Earrings

_ **Price:** $25.00 at shanalogic.com_
If you love the French hook dangle earrings above, but prefer studs, give these sweet little earrings a go instead! They feature the same 10-millimeter sterling silver disc with the same sweet sentiment. However, they sit close to your ear, and won’t get tangled in your tresses.

## 5. <3 Heart Necklace

_ **Price:** $20.00 at shanalogic.com_
This simple, sweet half-inch disc bears a message of modern love, the iconic “less than three” I’ve come to adore. It’s made of shining sterling silver and hangs by a jump ring from an 18-inch box chain. It’s ideal for layering with other silver necklaces… or just by itself as your everyday necklace. Either way, it's definitely a cute jewelry piece by Metal Smitten.

## 6. <3 Heart Dainty Ring

_ **Price:** $40.00 at shanalogic.com_
If you liked the band from item 2, but prefer something a little less subtle, this is the ring for you. It’s custom-made, in whole sizes from 4 to 9, from shiny sterling silver. The disc is slightly concave, stamped with the “less than three” symbol… I “less than three” it!

## 7. Sprouting Seedling Necklace

_ **Price:** $20.00 at shanalogic.com_
This necklace is so very cute, I had to include it even though it doesn’t feature the “less than three” symbol. Instead, this cute jewelry piece from Metal Smitten bears a simple sweet seedling, ideal for giving as a gift to the eco-warrior or gardener in your life. The half-inch pendant hangs from an 18-inch chain, and both are made of sterling silver.
