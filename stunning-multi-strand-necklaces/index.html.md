# 7 Stunning Multi Strand Necklaces ...

Multi Strand Necklaces are a stylish choice of accessory this season. They’re a great way of adding extra ‘oomph’ to an outfit. Collar and choker style lengths are an on-trend choice, while longer strand necklaces are timeless. You can go for heavily embellished multi strand necklaces or something more elegant and streamlined - the choice is up to you. Below are just a few multi strand necklaces for you to take inspiration from.

## 1. Topshop Knott Collar Necklace

![Topshop Knott Collar Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ka/rr/j22y9gvs54c932728333b107876445_310x400.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/81sDbv)
This necklace consists of five gold-look chains. It features an intricate knot detail at the front centre, which is the focal point of the entire necklace. It has a 20cm drop and has a fastening at the back. This multi strand necklace is quite bold and can be used to dress up a daytime look, or complement an edgy night time outfit.

## 2. Kenneth Jay Lane Glass-Pearl Multi Strand Necklace

![Kenneth Jay Lane Glass-Pearl Multi Strand Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/lo/jja2x4wc54c932739ad70032999016_267x392.jpg)

**Price:** $560.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/VKRhT0)
Pearl jewellery is timeless. Here, it has been given a contemporary twist in the form of a multi strand necklace. It consists of several strands of glass pearls, silver-tone hardware, and an adjustable hook fastening. Wear this necklace with a simple black shift dress for an elegant evening look.

## 3. Forever21 Two Toned Layered Necklace

_ **Price:** $10.80 at forever21.com_
A necklace with long layered strands is a great alternative to shorter, more embellished styles. This simple two-tone necklace will never go out of style. It features a high polish finish with Singapore chains and a lobster claw clasp. It is available in gold and black or gold and silver colourways.

## 4. Topshop Premium Cobweb Necklace

![Topshop Premium Cobweb Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hq/sz/wlul802654c932753f59c740447538_267x389.jpg)

This multi strand necklace is a stunner. It features black and gold-tone chains with a cobweb effect detailing. The necklace measures in at 72cm in length, so covers a fair bit of body. Keep the rest of your outfit simple and wear this necklace with a neutral tank top, cropped trousers and ballet flats.

## 5. Tom Binns Rhodium and Pearl Multi Strand Necklace

![Tom Binns Rhodium and Pearl Multi Strand Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/by/jm5w778f54c9327617085697451823_327x398.jpg)

**Price:** $1000.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ez5mfu)
This necklace combines classic materials such as pearls and Swarovski crystals, and incorporates them into a contemporary design. The neon pink safety pins are a very modern detail. The necklace strands are laced together to create a haphazard design. Add this necklace to a demure outfit for a playful twist.

## 6. Topshop Metal Tube Necklace

![Topshop Metal Tube Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/b8/onudij4y54c93277049e9022252571_320x400.jpg)

The strands vary in length, with the longest measuring approximately 22cm. The necklace has a certain tribal feel to it, and would look great against a printed blouse or dress.

## 7. Kenneth Jay Lane Multi Strand Beaded Necklace

![Kenneth Jay Lane Multi Strand Beaded Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/p0/ih68tyg954c93277edcb4031678025_339x400.jpg)

**Price:** $104.76 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/cyyjls)
This necklace is a stunner, if only for the number of strands that it incorporates into the design. This necklace features a multitude of beaded strands in a twist design. Ornate gold-look caps at the ends secure the strands. This necklace would look great worn with a colourful patterned blouse.

As you can see, the types of multi strand necklaces that are available vary. When wearing a multi strand necklace, particularly with thicker styles, it’s advisable to keep the rest of your jewellery to a minimum. However, if you think you can pull off a heavy array of adornments, then go for it! What are your thoughts on these multi strand necklaces?
