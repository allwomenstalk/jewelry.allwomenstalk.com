# 7 Beautiful Pieces from the Tiffany 1837 Collection ...

Tiffany and Company was founded in New York City in 1837, and to celebrate that pivotal moment in fine jewelry history, today Tiffany offers the 1987 collection, all classic pieces that will stand the test of time. Here are 7 beautiful pieces from the Tiffany 1987 collection…

## 1. Tiffany 1837 Interlocking Circles Bangle

_ **Price:** $2,500.00 at tiffany.com_
These two bangle bracelets are eternally bound, one in sterling silver and the other in 18-karat rose-colored gold. Each is stamped with the metal mark, the T & Co. signature, and the 1837 emblem. Each bangle will fit a wrist that measures up to 6¼” around.

## 2. Tiffany 1837 Interlocking Circles Lariat

_ **Price:** $975.00 at tiffany.com_
What a gorgeous necklace! I’d wear it with a low-cut LBD or with a cocktail dress. It’s made of bright 18-karat yellow gold and features a clever lariat design with three rings in total. Each ring is stamped with the T & Co. signature and the 1837 hallmark, and the necklace measures 18 inches long.

## 3. Tiffany 1837 Interlocking Circles Pendant

_ **Price:** $500.00 at tiffany.com_
How sweet! I love this necklace. It features two interlocking rings, one in silver, the other in 18-karat rose-colored gold. Each ring is stamped just like the other pieces in the collection, with the 1837 hallmark and the T & Co. signature. The necklace, including the pendant, measures a little more than 16 inches long.

## 4. Tiffany 1837 Ring

_ **Price:** $115.00 at tiffany.com_
This is something different! This ring is narrow, made of sleek, urban-chic midnight-black titanium. This would work for either a man or a woman, since it’s available in whole and half sizes from 4 to 13. Remember, since this is titanium, it can’t be sized, so be sure to buy the right size. Wouldn’t this make a gorgeous wedding band?

## 5. Tiffany 1837 Toggle Bracelet

_ **Price:** $225.00 at tiffany.com_
This sterling silver bracelet features chunky round links and a toggle closure stamped with the “925” metal mark, the T & Co. signature, and the 1837 hallmark. It’s ideal for everyday wear, and it looks smashing with almost every outfit you can dream up.

## 6. Tiffany 1837 Circle Pendant

_ **Price:** $125.00 at tiffany.com_
This simple, elegant pendant is suspended from a delicate 16-inch chain, perfect as your new everyday necklace. It’s made of sterling silver, and features one thin circle, stamped three times each with the “925” metal mark, the T & Co. signature, and the 1837 hallmark.

## 7. Tiffany 1837 Band Ring

_ **Price:** $200.00 at tiffany.com_
What a unique ring! It’s a simple band, made of bright sterling silver, stamped on the top, rather than the outside, with the metal mark, the T & Co. signature, and the 1837 hallmark. It’s available in whole and half sizes from 9 to 13… be sure to wear it with the markings on the top, not the bottom!

These are just a few of the dozens of gorgeous pieces in the Tiffany 1837 collection, and all of them are classics, pieces that can be worn for years and years… but they’re not boring or dull, they’re completely chic! I especially adore that titanium band, and the lariat necklace is so clever! Which of these pieces do you like best, and why?
