# 7 Good Luck Charms by Tiffany ...

You don’t have to be superstitious to want to add a little luck to your day, and what better way to do that than by wearing a favorite good luck charm? I’ve recently been searching for one, since I’m a little clumsy and more than a little absent-minded, and I’ve found some gorgeous ones at Tiffany, my favorite jeweler. Here are 7 good luck charms by Tiffany.

## 1. T&CO. Horseshoe Pendant

_ **Price:** $175.00 at tiffany.com_
This pendant features a classic good luck charm, a horseshoe, made of brilliant sterling silver, suspended from a 16-inch chain. It’s stamped with the T&Co. signature, and would make a marvelous graduation gift.

## 2. Tiffany Four-leaf Clover Tag Charm

_ **Price:** $100.00 at tiffany.com_
Add this bright sterling silver charm to your charm bracelet and see if you notice a change in your fortunes, or give it as a gift to bring a friend some luck. It’s easy to attach, with a spring-ring clasp at the top.

## 3. T&CO. Horseshoe Charm

_ **Price:** $400.00 at tiffany.com_
This 18-karat yellow gold charm is gorgeous, rich-looking… and ideal for making a statement while hopefully adding a little luck to your day. Wear it on a matching necklace, or add it to your charm bracelet. It hangs from a jaunty angle and bears the T&Co. signature.

## 4. Tiffany Chinese “Luck” Charm

_ **Price:** $675.00 at tiffany.com_
Made of shining 18-karat yellow gold and sleek red enamel, this charm carries the Chinese character for “luck,” bestowing upon its wearer good fortune. The charm is tiny, but very detailed and gorgeous, and would look smashing on a matching chain or bracelet. I’d wear it on a necklace, and layer it with other gold necklaces for a dazzling look.

## 5. T&CO. Horseshoe Cuff Links

_ **Price:** $225.00 at tiffany.com_
Hey, guys need good luck too, so why not give your sweetie these cuff links, made of shining sterling silver, each stamped with the classic T&Co. signature. Then he can add a little luck at job interviews, client meetings, and more…

## 6. Tiffany Keys Clover Key Pendant

![Tiffany Keys Clover Key Pendant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bl/fv/pz18jt6j54c8f6a9ace03696288824_475x273.jpg)

**Price:** $1,250.00 at [tiffany.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tiffany.com/Shopping/Item.aspx?fromGrid=1&sku=25760654&mcat=&cid=&search_params=s+1-p+2-c+-r+-x+-n+6-ri+-ni+0-t+clover-k+&search=1)
A three-leaf clover may not be as lucky as a four-leaf clover, but it is still sumptuously gorgeous, especially in the center of this pendant from the Tiffany Keys collection. It’s made of 18-karat yellow gold and set with three round-cut diamonds with a total carat weight of 0.02. It measures more than 2 inches long and can be purchased alone or with a matching chain.

## 7. Tiffany Horseshoe Pendant

_ **Price:** $2,250.00 at tiffany.com_
If you liked the horseshoe pendant from item 1, but want something with a little more sparkle and shine, then this is the good luck charm for you! It’s made of gleaming platinum, with 18 round-cut brilliant diamonds for a total carat weight of 0.20 on a platinum chain which measures 16 inches long. How gorgeous! Anyone who wears this is a lucky lady indeed!

With so many gorgeous, glittery good luck charms to choose from, you’re bound to find fortune at every turn! Which of these good luck charms by Tiffany do you like best, and how would you use it to improve your luck? Please share!
