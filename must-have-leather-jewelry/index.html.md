# 7 Must\-Have Leather Jewelry ...

Leather jewelry provides an interesting alternative to normal metal jewelry. The look is slightly more casual and carefree. Jewelry with leather straps and embellishments can really add interest to an outfit. Lighter tan leathers can give outfits a slight boho feel, while black leather jewelry adds a bit of toughness to an outfit. Take a look at some must-have leather jewelry below.

## 1. Chan Luu Sterling Silver and Leather Wrap Bracelet

![Chan Luu Sterling Silver and Leather Wrap Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k9/yt/y9t7090054c906c4e2b75691557539_400x120.jpg)

**Price:** $180.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/vbCDpC)
This simple wrap bracelet features a charming silver wing embellishment. The strap is made from black leather and features silver-tipped leather self ties. Simply wrap this bracelet and fasten it around your wrist. It’s bound to add a bit of a bohemian edge to any outfit.

## 2. Fallon Labryinth Knot Ring

_ **Price:** $98.00 at barneys.com_
This piece of must-have leather jewelry almost looks like a weapon. This ring feature gold-plated hardware and a knotted leather cord. The tips of the leather cord are finished with gold spike embellishments. Wear this ring with a fairly basic outfit so as to let it really stand out.

## 3. Alexander McQueen Cracked Leather Skull Bracelet

![Alexander McQueen Cracked Leather Skull Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kh/n0/dp7bmtu554c906c6d0b86976022124_400x236.jpg)

**Price:** $250.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/MVsNUa)
This leather bracelet has a metallic-gold cracked finish. It’s a bit of a more glamorous take on the leather jewelry trend. This bracelet features a Swarovski crystal embellished skull charm and gold buckle fastening. Team this bracelet with a timeless outfit of silk blouse and jeans, and pair it with your regular gold jewelry.

## 4. ACB Lineae Embellished Leather Necklace

![ACB Lineae Embellished Leather Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/kl/g4hl9w2q54c906c7e02fb712578480_267x400.jpg)

**Price:** $370.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/fToS0Z)
This piece of must-have leather jewelry is very much tribal-inspired. This leather-covered chain necklace features large tusk-shaped charms at the drop. The charms are attached to a cotton cord which is also embellished with brass tubes and rings. As this is quite a statement necklace, keep the rest of your jewelry fairly minimal.

## 5. Giles & Brother Hook 10-Karat Gold-Plated Leather Wrap Bracelet

![Giles & Brother Hook 10-Karat Gold-Plated Leather Wrap Bracelet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uj/n3/scqqzm3j54c906c929496388345759_400x397.jpg)

**Price:** $95.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/SD6B81)
Leather wrap bracelets are the simplest form of leather jewelry. This light brown leather bracelet features 10-karat gold-plated hook and ring embellishments. The hook not only acts as a decoration but as the fastening point of the bracelet. This would look great worn with a light sundress and wrap sandals.

## 6. Maison Martin Margiela Line 11 Ostrich Cuff

![Maison Martin Margiela Line 11 Ostrich Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pn/o7/b19hargu54c906ca21230793934048_452x314.jpg)

**Price:** $235.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This chic leather cuff is a contemporary take on the leather jewelry trend. It is made from ostrich leather and features rounded ends with a silver snap closure. The exterior of the cuff has a textured finish while the interior is smooth. The cuff has two fasteners which allows for an adjustable fit.

## 7. Fendi Stitched Bracelet

_ **Price:** $120.00 at barneys.com_
This leather bracelet is a casually chic piece of must-have leather jewelry. This bracelet features contrasting stitching that runs through the centre of the leather. It also has designer stamped silver-tone hardware. Slip this on your wrist for some instant street-cred.

One of the easiest ways to wear leather jewelry is around the wrist. However, you can also find leather jewelry in the form of necklaces and rings. Which of these pieces of must-have leather jewelry do you like best?
