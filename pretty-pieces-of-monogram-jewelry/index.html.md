# 7 Pretty Pieces of Monogram Jewelry ...

Since I was a little girl, I loved collecting unusual jewelry with my initial on it, from charms to earrings to pendants and more. Now I’ve matured a little beyond the “J” string friendship bracelet (not really), into more sophisticated, grown-up girl monogram jewelry. It’s not like my initial (or name) is so unique, but I just like how monogram jewelry looks… here are 7 pretty pieces of monogram jewelry from Tiffany to help prove my point...

## 1. Tiffany Alphabet Heart Tag Bracelet

_ **Price:** $230.00 at tiffany.com_
How pretty! This open round-link bracelet has one single charm, heart-shaped and stamped with your initial. The bracelet (which measures 7½ inches long) and medium-sized charm are both made of bright sterling silver. It’s an exclusive to the Tiffany website, so you won’t find it in stores.

## 2. Tiffany Letters Pendant

_ **Price:** $1,300.00 at tiffany.com_
Diamonds are, after all, a girl’s best friend, so why not opt for monogram jewelry that’s covered in carats? Choose your initial, then wear the glittering thing for every day or for dressy affairs. The round-cut diamonds measure a total carat weight of 0.08.

## 3. Elsa Peretti Letter “a” Pendant

_ **Price:** $425.00 at tiffany.com_
Tiffany designer Elsa Peretti offers a myriad of monogram possibilities with her sweetly rounded cursive letters, all in lower case. Choose this version, in sterling silver with 0.02 carats of round-cut diamonds, or silver without stones, or 18k gold without stones. Each initial pendant comes with a delicate 16-inch chain to match.

## 4. Tiffany Notes Letter “a” Disc Charm

_ **Price:** $300.00 at tiffany.com_
This round, flat disc comes engraved with your initial, inspired by natural hand-writing (tough mine’s not nearly this pretty). The disc is made of 18 karat yellow gold, but it’s also available in bright sterling silver.

## 5. Tiffany Letter Lock Charm Pendant

_ **Price:** $150.00 at tiffany.com_
These monogram charms remind me of the wooden letter blocks I had when I was little. Each block letter is added to a small working lock in sterling silver. Choose your initial from the drop-down box to see exactly what it will look like before you add it to your shopping basket.

## 6. Elsa Peretti Alphabet Pendant with Open Heart Charm

_ **Price:** $520.00 at tiffany.com_
What a sweet twist on traditional monogram jewelry! Start with your initial, or a loved one’s initial, then add to it the Else Peretti Open Heart charm in 18 karat rose gold. String them together on a delicate 16 inch chain… how adorable!

## 7. Letters “X” & “O” Lock Charm Pendant

_ **Price:** $300.00 at tiffany.com_
**Here’s another monogram jewelry twist:** use the letters to send hugs and kisses, with an O and an X together on the same chain! You can use the Else Peretti initials, or these, the Tiffany Letter Locks. Either way, wouldn’t it make a sweet birthday gift?

So whether your name is Ann or Zelda, wear your monogram jewelry proudly, especially if it’s as pretty as these pieces! I especially adore the lower-case cursive letters… which of these pretty pieces of monogram jewelry do you like best, and why? Which initial would you choose? Please share!
