# Sexy Style Inspo for Women Who Want Their Men to Rock the Bling !

Jewelry isn't reserved for women. There are men that can pull off even the most "feminine" accessories and make them look hotter than ever. So if you can't figure out what to buy your boyfriend for his birthday or your anniversary, ask yourself if you think he'd look hot while wearing one of the things on this list. The chances are that these accessories will [look sexy](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-women-who-want-to-look-super-sexy-without-looking-sleazy/) on any man:

## 1. Necklaces

![Necklaces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hw/bm/rpukramr55c3a308bef51687778065_600x836.jpg)

When a guy like Dean Winchester wears a necklace, he never wants to take it off. Unlike most people, who tend to wear new accessories every single day to match their outfits, guys like Dean have a deep connection with what they're wearing. Maybe their grandmother or their brother gave them the necklace as a gift and they're wearing it in honor of them. Either way, they look sexy, so the story behind it doesn't matter.

## 2. Watches

![Watches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tw/a4/mmtuqzlq55c3a392a5dc1059129789_539x810.jpg)

For some reason, watches look manly, especially when a beefcake has one strapped onto his strong arms. Plus, watches suggest that a man is responsible and can take care of himself. They show that he actually cares about getting to work on time and that he'd never leave a lady waiting on a date. A guy with a watch is a guy you can rely on.

## 3. Earrings

![Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l8/ks/kgjnpb1555c3a3bc6cffd430109507_500x750.jpg)

Some men only wear a piercing in one ear. Other men wear them in both ears. Daring (and some would say, crazy) men will even wear gauges that open up the holes in their ears. No matter what style they're rocking, it can look sexy on any man .

## 4. Alternate Piercings

![Alternate Piercings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/xd/xgxa7sgf55c3a3fde7816722550058_481x700.jpg)

Sometimes, ear piercings aren't enough. That's why there are plenty of men with eyebrow rings, lip rings, and nose rings--and most of them look absolutely amazing with them. The right piercing can draw attention to a guy's best features, so the piercings will only make him appear sexier.

## 5. Rings

![Rings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s3/g4/h3xbsnd555c3a431b0dae043854627_427x640.jpg)

There's nothing sexier than seeing a man wear a wedding ring. It shows that he's devoted to his wife and wants everyone to know that he's taken. Of course, that's not the only type of ring that looks hot on a man. Whether he wants to wear his school ring, a football ring, or a ring he bought from the mall, he'll be able to rock it.

## 6. Bracelets

![Bracelets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/sh/o9d881be55c3a4899afb5968135603_600x900.jpg)

Any type of man can wear a bracelet, even if he isn't typically interested in jewelry. If he's a surfer dude, he can wear a rope bracelet. If he's in a band, he can wear leather bracelets and chains. If he doesn't really fit into any category, he can wear a rubber wristband to support a charity. No matter what a guy is interested in, he can find a bracelet to fit his personality.

## 7. Glasses

![Glasses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pf/c1/ru3jz73655c3a4be06aef845299480_373x560.jpg)

Looking "nerdy" is in nowadays. Even though glasses used to be considered unfashionable, now they're worn as fashion pieces. Whether or not a man has issues with his eyes, it's always nice to see him wearing a sexy pair of glasses.

There's no reason for male and female fashion to be all that different, because guys can look sexy in the same items that we wear. Does your boyfriend wear any accessories or would he refuse to even try them on?
