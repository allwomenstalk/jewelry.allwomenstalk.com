## How To Style Your Maya Jewelry Picks

Are you looking to make a collection of these dazzling jewels your own? Here are some styling tips that might help bring out your inner trendsetter:

-

**Mix and Match Metals:** Don’t shy away from combining gold with silver or rose gold pieces. This mix-and-match approach creates a chic and modern layered look.

-

**Let Your Ears Do the Talking:** With pieces like those in this collection, it’s easy to let your ears become your statement accessory. Opt for minimalist clothing to let your ear jewelry take the limelight.

-

**Balance is Key:** If you’re going big on the earrings, consider toning down other accessories to keep the focus on your ears without overwhelming your overall look.

-

**Keep It Personal:** Incorporate pieces that reflect your personality, like a birthstone accent or a charm that holds special meaning. It's _your_ style story, after all.

While Ashley showcased how to pull off a _bold and elegant look_, your personal style can guide you to reveal your unique take on these beautiful pieces. The essence of Maya Jewelry lies in its ability to blend seamlessly into the narrative of your own life.
