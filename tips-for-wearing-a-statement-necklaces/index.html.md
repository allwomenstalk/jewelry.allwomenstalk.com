# 7 Tips for Wearing a Statement Necklaces ...

If you want your outfit to really make a statement, toss on a statement necklace. They come in tons of different sizes and shapes, so you'll definitely find a few you love. If you're not sure what you'd wear them with, follow _BHG's_ advice.

## 1. Wear with V-Necks

![Wear with V-Necks](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/j8/yeuz4h6h562f7832ba1de403424789_550x611.jpg)

They can look amazing with v-necks.

## 2. Wear with Collared Shirts

![Wear with Collared Shirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hp/zt/lwelhtte562f7832e9e24305070174_550x733.jpg)

You won't look any less professional with a statement necklace. It can actually enhance your outfit.

## 3. Wear Two at Once

![Wear Two at Once](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xl/lm/nn7ovxpb562f7833186c4413487092_550x733.jpg)

If one looks too bland, you can layer them together. Just don't get the chains tangled.

## 4. Wear Colorful Necklaces

![Wear Colorful Necklaces](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ey/oi98vsbf562f78337d5b2136784942_550x733.jpg)

Don't be afraid to wear bold shades. Statement necklaces can come in some pretty funky colors, so use them to your advantage.

## 5. Don't Wear Too Much Jewelry at Once

![Don't Wear Too Much Jewelry at Once](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/ay/mw3bv2w9562f7833985ba770121886_550x611.jpg)

If you're going to wear an attention grabbing necklace, don't wear too many bracelets or earrings with it. You want to wear your jewelry in moderation.

## 6. Wear with High Necklines

![Wear with High Necklines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/od/vx/gmwtobv7562f7833a2a20196058508_550x733.jpg)

This is the best type of shirt to wear a statement necklace with! Of course, if you want others to notice the jewelry, you might want to wear your hair up. It'll give you a more sophisticated look.

## 7. Wear with Stripes

![Wear with Stripes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x7/s9/papn3hw7562f7833e5bd0091576974_550x611.jpg)

They look great with stripes, because it breaks up the pattern of your shirt. It'll really make your necklace pop.
