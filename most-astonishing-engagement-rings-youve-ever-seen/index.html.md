# 21 Most Astonishing Engagement Rings Youve Ever Seen ...

Who hasn't dreamt of a gorgeous glittering engagement ring, one measured in carats you can't even count? In real life, sure, my style is a lot less bling-y, but in my dreams, any of these (almost) over-the-top engagement rings will do.

## 1. Tiffany Soleste® round

_ Price: From $62,500 at tiffany.com_
This sumptuous ring features one gorgeous round-cut diamond surrounded by dual bands of pink and white bead-cut stones.

## 2. Tiffany round Brilliant Ribbon Ring

_ Price: $14,700 at tiffany.com_
The round-cut diamond in the center is expected, sure, but it's surrounded by two vertical bands of bead-cut stones, giving it a uniquely elegant look.

## 3. Tiffany Three Stone Ring with Sapphire Side Stones

_ Price: TBD at tiffany.com_

_ Price: $14,800 at tiffany.com_

## 5. Tiffany & Co. Schlumberger® Two Bees Ring

_ Price: $68,900 at tiffany.com_
