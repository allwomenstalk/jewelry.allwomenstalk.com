# 8 Eye\-Catching Oversized Earrings ...

Oversized earrings are the way to go if you want to make a statement with your jewellery. When choosing an oversized earring design, the sky’s the limit really. There are just so many options from classic gold metals to funkier plastics and neon colours. Take a look at a few oversized style earrings below and remember that bigger is definitely better this season.

## 1. ASOS Bauble Interlink Circle Drop Earrings

![ASOS Bauble Interlink Circle Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/1_asos-bauble-interlink-circle-drop-earrings_600x705.jpg)

**Price:** $33.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/nZfKm0)
These oversized earrings have a bit of a gaudy eighties vibe to them. Nevertheless, they’re perfect for making a statement in. They have an interlinked dome design and come in a gold tone metal finish. Team them with a pussy bow blouse and blazer for a modern take on power dressing.

## 2. ASOS Zig Zag Earrings

![ASOS Zig Zag Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/2_asos-zig-zag-earrings_600x679.jpg)

**Price:** $18.18 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/Vc0F5f).
There’s been a trend towards bold, geometric lines when it comes to both fashion and jewellery lately. These earrings have a large triangle shape and feature enamelled zig zag detailing throughout. Show these off by tying your hair up in a high pony. Wear them with a colourful top during the day or with a chic black dress at night.

## 3. Topshop Smooth Zig Zag Earrings

![Topshop Smooth Zig Zag Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/3_topshop-smooth-zig-zag-earrings_600x759.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/WSQV2J)
Art Deco styles are everywhere when it comes to fashion at the moment. This might have something to do with the vintage/retro revival that is happening. Get in on the trend with these lovely pastel green earrings. Complete the look by teaming these oversized earrings with a drop waist dress and small embellished sling bag.

## 4. ASOS Neon Trip Drop Earrings

![ASOS Neon Trip Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/4_asos-neon-trip-drop-earrings_600x807.jpg)

**Price:** $22.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/NyBggr).
Whether you’re a fan of these or not, you can’t deny the fact that they are quite eye-catching. Neon colours are still a popular choice, and neon jewellery is quite a novelty. These earrings are made from acrylic and feature neon yellow, orange, and green shapes. Keep the rest of your outfit fairly neutral to prevent looking like Rainbow Brite!

## 5. Kenneth Jay Lane Beaded Tassel Earrings

![Kenneth Jay Lane Beaded Tassel Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/5_kenneth-jay-lane-beaded-tassel-earrings_196x560.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/R40UBp)
Oversized earrings that skim the shoulders can be elegantly breathtaking. These particular statement earrings feature green and jade-effect resin beads and are finished off with black nylon tassels. To really show these off, wear them with a shoulder baring dress or top.

## 6. ASOS Folded Square Stud Earring

![ASOS Folded Square Stud Earring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/6_asos-folded-square-stud-earring_600x454.jpg)

**Price:** $9.09 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/33FPvJ).
For a more subtle take on the oversized earring trend, try these stud earrings. They have a folded square design and come in a sleek gold-tone metal finish. These are the kind of statement earrings that you could wear on a daily basis and not just for special occasions.

## 7. Topshop Circle Triangle Drop Earrings

![Topshop Circle Triangle Drop Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jewelry/2012/04/7_topshop-circle-triangle-drop-earrings_600x876.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/IOs32q)
These oversized earrings combine classic metal finishing with colour. They feature a gold-look circle with a yellow triangle at the drop. The design is quite contemporary. Wear these with a white blouse and jeans for a simple yet stylish weekend look.

## 8. Pieces Ziangle Statement Earrings

_ **Price:** $13.00 at [us.asos.com](https://goo.gl/CClQb5).
Rose gold jewellery is having a trending moment. Work this trend in the form of oversized earrings to really make a statement. These earrings have an angled triangle drop design and would look great worn with a neutral shirt dress.

Statement earrings are a great way to add some extra detail around your face. Since oversized earrings can have to potential to be quite heavy and cumbersome, keep an eye out for more lightweight styles. What are your thoughts on the oversized earring this season?
