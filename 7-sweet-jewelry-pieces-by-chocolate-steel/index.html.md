# 7 Sweet Jewelry Pieces by Chocolate & Steel ...

I love silver jewelry, and have a short list of designers I love to browse each week, including Chocolate & Steel, and indie shop with the most adorable rings, necklaces, and more. All of their jewelry is hand-crafted with care and love, and it shows. Here are 7 sweet jewelry pieces by Chocolate & Steel.

## 1. Fields of Dandelions Ring

_ **Price:** $48.00 at shanalogic.com_
Made of bright reclaimed sterling silver, this wide band ring has been gently hammered for texture, then stamped with a field full of fluffy dandelions, ready for making wishes. Choose your ring size from 4 to 12 (in whole sizes) then wait a few weeks for it to be custom-made just for you!

## 2. Little Happy Snail Necklace

_ **Price:** $36.00 at shanalogic.com_
This sweet smiling snail is looking for a new home, and a new friend… maybe it can be you? He’s made of reclaimed sterling silver, and dangles delicately from a shiny silver. If you fall in love with him, and of course you will, there’s a matching ring with his twin as well.

## 3. Silver Heart Stud Earrings

_ **Price:** $20.00 at shanalogic.com_
Aren’t these sweet? These dainty sterling silver studs measure 7 millimeters across and each is stamped with one tiny heart, ideal for celebrating your love on Valentine’s Day, or any day you need a little romance. They’re light-weight and comfortable, and oh-so-darling!

## 4. Silver Bird Stud Earrings

_ **Price:** $20.00 at shanalogic.com_
Welcome spring with these charming little chirpies, stamped into reclaimed sterling silver posts about a half-inch across. Pair with a few long necklaces, and a cute hair bow, and you have a look that’s ready to go with a floral-print dress.

## 5. Bird Silhouette Necklace

_ **Price:** $38.00 at shanalogic.com_
This rectangular pendant features an Eames-inspired bird silhouette, simple and stark and elegant. It hands from a delicate 18-inch sterling silver chain. Layer it with other, longer silver-tone necklaces, or wear it alone with a vee-neck sweater or dress.

## 6. Silver Pear Stacking Ring

_ **Price:** $40.00 at shanalogic.com_
What a marvelously unique little ring! It features one perfect pear, stamped into a flat disc attached to a thin silver band. Wear it alone or stacked with other silver rings… either way, it will look chic and sweet. Choose your size from 4 to 9, and your ring will be custom-made within just a few weeks.

## 7. Silver Heart Stacking Ring

_ **Price:** $40.00 at shanalogic.com_
Remember those darling little heart stud earrings from item 3, above? This is the ring that matches them, with the same sweet heart stamped into reclaimed sterling silver, on a thin silver band. It’s ideal for stacking with other rings, like the one with the pear, above, or wear it alone. So romantic!

These are just a few of the pieces that Chocolate & Steel offer, and their line includes so many more darling necklaces, rings, and earrings… but these are my favorite! I especially love that little snail pendant… he’s so cute! Which of these pieces by Chocolate & Steel do you like best, and why? Please share!
