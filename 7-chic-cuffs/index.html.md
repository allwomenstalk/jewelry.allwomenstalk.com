# 7 Chic Cuffs ...

Cuffs are a chic way of accessorising an outfit. Unlike bracelets and bangles, cuffs often have an opening and are structurally solid. They can come in a range of design, sizes, and materials. They can be worn loose on the wrist or pushed further up the arm. Below are a few chic cuffs for you to check out.

## 1. Etro Hammered Gold-Plated Cuff

![Etro Hammered Gold-Plated Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/hz/v72wx5el54c8fc5a12ad8246930802_400x469.jpg)

**Price:** $375.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/uDDMnj)
This gold-plated brass cuff would make an eye-catching accessory. It has hinged wrap effect panels and hammered detailing. The size of this cuff covers a substantial area of the forearm. As the cuff is quite heavy in appearance, try pairing it with finer and more delicate metallic accents.

## 2. Topshop Cut out Large Cuff

![Topshop Cut out Large Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/o3/tnqkhf5l54c8fc5b4674a149684338_400x365.jpg)

**Price:** $28.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/g1E2A6)
This cut out cuff almost looks like an antique. It has a slight art-deco vibe about it. The design features intricate curved patterns and a gold-look finish. Large chic cuffs such as this one would look stunning paired with a flowing maxi dress.

## 3. Asos Triple Bow Cuff

![Asos Triple Bow Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rc/rs/ip3zfzdh54c8fc5c550dd453876522_400x285.jpg)

**Price:** $17.93 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/xcjFCQ)
This leather-look cuff has a playful design. It has a wide band and features three bows along the front. Snap button closures line the reverse of the cuff. This cuff would be well suited to those who prefer not to wear traditional jewellery. Pair this with a casual day dress, or with a loose fitting tee and tailored shorts.

## 4. Giles & Brother Hook Gold-Plated Cuff

![Giles & Brother Hook Gold-Plated Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s0/cg/vz2h7avy54c8fc5d10cde523825369_400x150.jpg)

**Price:** $115.00 at [net-a-porter.com](https://goo.gl/ASvBxu)
This 10-karat gold-plated cuff is a subtle and unassuming. It has a curved ‘hook’ design and features a discreet designer signature at the hook. Chic cuffs such as these can easily be incorporated into your day to day jewellery collection. Wear it alone for a demure fuss-free look, or stack it with more jewellery for something a bit more eclectic.

## 5. Wendy Nichol Devil’s Eye Cuff

![Wendy Nichol Devil’s Eye Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kf/my/v6r1yzki54c8fc5dd054a931746746_400x144.jpg)

**Price:** $380.00 at [lagarconne.com](http://www.lagarconne.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cuff has a certain tribal feel to it. It is fashioned from solid brass and features two black diamond ‘eyes’ at the centre. The engraved line and triangle designs lend to its tribal aesthetic. This cuff would make an excellent accessory to an outfit consisting of a black maxi skirt, cropped singlet and floppy felt hat.

## 6. Topshop Metal Loop Cuff

![Topshop Metal Loop Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bt/xq/s81ipbnh54c8fc5ea73ff276026353_400x324.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 at [us.topshop.com](https://goo.gl/txXE0i)
If gold jewellery isn’t your thing, take a look at this silver-tone metal cuff. The design is graphic and modern. It has a loop design and features curved engraving throughout. This cuff would work well with a range of casual and evening looks.

## 7. Asos Wraparound Leaf Arm Cuff

![Asos Wraparound Leaf Arm Cuff](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/nb/p5z64jlv54c8fc5f7e810644119845_400x135.jpg)

**Price:** $17.93 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/TDI30Y)
This wraparound cuff could be worn loose around the forearm or pushed up on the arm (for a bit of a bohemian twist). It has a gold-tone finish and engraved leaf detailing at each end. The wraparound design sets is apart from the rest of these chic cuffs. This cuff could easily be incorporated into a range of outfits.

As you can see, there are a large variety of cuffs available. Which of the above chic cuffs do you like best?
