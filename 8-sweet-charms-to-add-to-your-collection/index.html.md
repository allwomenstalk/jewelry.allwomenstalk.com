# 8 Sweet Charms to Add to Your Collection ...

Charms are an easy way to update your look. They can add character and sentiment to an outfit. Wear them around your neck, on a bracelet, or on your handbag. There are just so many options!

## 1. Juicy Couture Pink Sewing Machine Charm

![Juicy Couture Pink Sewing Machine Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/l1/qjotonxc54c8f32e44791647113297_195x238.jpg)

**Price:** $103.99 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/IpXg30)
This drop charm is the cutest sewing machine I’ve ever seen. It features logo lettering and a lobster claw clasp. I love the attention to detail such as the diamante wheel. The gold tone accents look great against the soft pink body. I think this charm would look absolutely adorable on a chain necklace!

## 2. Links of London Silver Snowflake Charm

![Links of London Silver Snowflake Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/xg/lrymg82u54c8f32f1e44d748871371_138x245.jpg)

**Price:** $80.69 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/O1xTB3)
This is snowflake shaped charm is so elegant. It’s made from sterling silver and comes in a branded box. This charm will definitely add a unique aspect to your outfit. It would look perfect dangling on a long chain or added to a charm bracelet.

## 3. Juicy Couture Crystal Heart & Bow Handbag Charm

![Juicy Couture Crystal Heart & Bow Handbag Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yb/eg/i72vnbd254c8f32fdfbbb623817349_135x248.jpg)

**Price:** $103.99 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/HzC9H1)
This charm is designed to be worn on your handbag. The charm contains gold-tone links and bow feature. The faceted crystal heart is my favourite design feature. This charm is a great way to add a touch of individuality to your look.

## 4. Tiffany & Co. Blue Box Charm

_ **Price:** $200.00 at tiffany.com_
This charm is designed to emulate the signature Tiffany Blue Box. It is designed in sterling silver with a Tiffany Blue enamel finish. I am a huge fan of this charm! I think this would look perfect on a Tiffany bracelet, alongside other charms.

## 5. Juicy Couture Frappe Charm

![Juicy Couture Frappe Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/he/j1wee6d654c8f33165909671552648_170x245.jpg)

**Price:** $48.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you love your frappes, then showcase it by wearing this little charm. I love the realistic design. The whipped cream topping and silver straw are cute details. Just note that it’s not really edible!

## 6. Links of London Silver Butterfly Charm

![Links of London Silver Butterfly Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/d2/ekasbtrd54c8f33219ae5669622441_163x237.jpg)

**Price:** $62.76 at [asos.com](https://goo.gl/BAHkwv)
This sterling silver butterfly charm is just gorgeous. The wings are ridged on the front side and smooth on the back. It’s a simple yet stylish design. This is definitely one to add to your charm bracelet.

## 7. Tiffany & Co. Envelope Charm

_ **Price:** $200.00 at tiffany.com_
This charm is the sweetest. It’s made from sterling silver and is designed in the shape of an envelope. It’s addressed to Tiffany & Co. in New York and features a round diamond in place of a stamp! I’d be tempted to wear this charm on a daily basis.

## 8. Tiffany & Co. Cupcake Charm

![Tiffany & Co. Cupcake Charm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/df/fj9yvxc154c8f333a1129366334045_160x265.jpg)

**Price:** $175.00 at [tiffany.com](https://web.archive.org/web/http://www.tiffany.com/shopping/Item.aspx?fromGrid=1&sku=23019493&mcat=148204&cid=288216)
Treat yourself with this [cute cupcake](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/of-the-cutest-cupcakes-youll-ever-see/) charm from Tiffany & Co. It comes in sterling silver with a pink enamel finish. I love the flecks of silver peeking through the pink ‘icing’. This would look great on a silver chain or bracelet.
